jackie's parasite tungiasis tungiasis the biology treatment & public health sources & links tungiasis is an infestation of the skin by the sand flea tunga penetrans the pregnant female flea burrows under the skin sucks blood swells and releases eggs a localized nodule will form at the point of infestation possibly causing pain and inflammation complications though rare may involve ulceration gangrene secondary infection tetanus and death this burrowing flea is endemic to latin america the caribbean africa and india

treatment of sarcocystosis treatment there is no specific treatment for human sarcocystis infection symptoms are generally self limiting and mild however some authors have suggested the use of certain drugs examples are albendazole for suppressing chronic symptoms of muscular sarcocystis infection metronidazole and cotrimoxazole antibacterial for eosinophilic myositis corticosteroids to reduce inflammation associated with muscular involvement copyright 2004 stephanie adams prepared for parasites and pestilence dr scott smith created 23 may 2004

guidelines we should strive to walk 10000 steps approximately 5 miles a day depending on your current level of activity that might seem like a daunting goal however by setting and meeting incremental goals eventually you will reach 10000 steps a day it just takes time the good news is that as little as 30 minutes of moderate physical activity a day will bring health benefits in fact any increase in your physical activity will improve your health this self directed online class will help you gradually increase your daily steps and your overall level of physical activity it's as easy as wearing a pedometer and recording your daily steps the $25 class fee includes a pedometer a week by week activity and step journal booklet weekly e mailed motivational tips on line progress reports and graphs

when the surfaces that are being scanned are too dark or occluded the laser scanner does not return any data as a result you may encounter holes in our models fragment 010f to make our models more visually pleasing we have filled the smaller holes with an artificial surface using an automated process most of the time this holefilling is unnoticeable to the user and does not create any false impressions about the original fragment since the artificial surface follows the geometry of the model however when a particularly large hole is filled a smooth piece of surface is created that may be very different from the original fragment the image on the left shows a hole from an iron bar filled with a smooth surface these artifacts are particularly easy to spot and ignore since the holefilling surface is a lot smoother than the geometry that surrounds it back to top natasha gelfand 4 1 2004

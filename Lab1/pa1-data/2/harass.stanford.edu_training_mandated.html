directions mandated training for faculty and supervisors california law ab1825 mandates that all supervisors receive a minimum of two hours of harassment prevention training at least every two years under california's broad legal definition of supervisor all faculty members qualify as supervisors as do all staff members who supervise other employees even if they supervise student or temporary workers the university cannot exempt any supervisors faculty or staff from this legal obligation if you have not completed training by your stated deadline you may not be able to remain in a supervisory role if you are a recently hired staff supervisor or faculty member or have recently been promoted to a supervisory position please contact your human resources office or your faculty affairs officer or administrator and provide the following information first and last name whether you are staff or faculty division school department job title email address hire date sunetid the hr or faculty affairs personnel will need to contact the sexual harassment policy office at shpo training stanford edu to confirm your supervisory status and provide us with the above information if you require any accommodations to participate in this training please contact our office learn more about who must be trained find out about online training other questions consult our list of faqs

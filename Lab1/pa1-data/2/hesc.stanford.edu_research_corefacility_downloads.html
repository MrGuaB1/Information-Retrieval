lines to stanford researchers stanford and wicell have signed a memorandum of understanding mou under which wicell can transfer human embryonic stem cells to stanford researchers this mou also allows stanford researchers to distribute wicell lines to their fellow stanford researchers in order for a stanford researcher to provide wicell lines to you you need to complete sign and if you are not a principal investigator have your pi sign and submit the following forms 1 stanford incoming mta routing form please complete and sign or have your pi sign and then fax email to the stanford industrial contracts office ico at 725 7295 or ico otlmail stanford edu this form provides information ico needs about your research download 2 wicell simple letter agreement sla please complete and sign or have your pi sign two slas for each line requested please send originals to ico wicell requires this form for each cell line you receive download 3 wicell annual certification please complete and sign or have your pi sign and send original to ico wicell requires this certification download after ico has received your forms and confirmed with the scro manager that your protocol using the line has been approved it will sign the sla and send it back to wicell along with the annual certification form once ico receives the fully executed sla back from wicell it will notify the institute that you may receive the line and send you a copy of the sla if you have any questions please feel free to contact the stanford university institute for stem cell biology and regenerative medicine hesc_core

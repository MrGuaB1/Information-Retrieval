departments in the school of medicine it was created in the late seventies by john w farquhar md its mission is to improve individual and population health by developing and disseminating effective health promotion and chronic disease prevention and management strategies through interdisciplinary research education and community outreach the center conducts problem focused research using mainly experimental methods to test and diffuse disease prevention and control programs it uses collaboration among a broad array of health professionals and social and behavioral scientists who share a

stanford students the vaden allergy immunization and injection clinic offers safe continuous care for students receiving allergy injections as ordered by their allergy specialist there is a charge for each allergy clinic appointment prior to receiving your first injection we require the following information allergy specialist's order including duration of immunotherapy and the current progression or injection schedule towards maintenance your specific diagnosis a copy of your antigen extract prescription antigen for injection labeled with patient name content concentration and expiration date

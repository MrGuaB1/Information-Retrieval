creative solutions to problems 16 mar 1999 creative solutions to problems the idea is to chip a piece out of the problem of creativity by defining a emph creative solution to a problem relative to the functions and predicates used in posing the problem the simplification comes from not talking about the creativity of the problem solver but only about the creativity of the solution definition informal a solution to a problem is creative if it involves concepts not present in statement of the problem and the general knowledge surrounding it don t identify creativity with difficulty although they are usually correlated example the mutilated checkerboard problem we also consider how to express concisely the idea of a solution whether the expression is adequate is relative to the knowledge and ability of the person or program to which the idea is expressed this paper is for the aisb workshop on ai and scientific creativity edinburgh 1999 april 8 and 9 there are html dvi ps and pdf versions up to mccarthy home page send comments to jmc cs stanford edu the number of hits on this page since 1999 march 17

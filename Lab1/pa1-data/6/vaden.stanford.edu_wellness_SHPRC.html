sexual health resources and peer counseling to stanford students and to promoting safe healthy relationships across campus it is entirely student run and funded and all of the supplies and information need to know about sex and sexuality products and resources the shprc offers all registered stanford undergraduate students with id $2 worth of free products each quarter this can be applied to all of our products including condoms lube samples female condoms dental dams and early pregnancy tests epts all stanford students may purchase materials from the center at reduced cost the shprc also has a vast library of books that may be checked out with a valid stanford id and a cash deposit prescription items are not available through the center but are available through vaden student health center

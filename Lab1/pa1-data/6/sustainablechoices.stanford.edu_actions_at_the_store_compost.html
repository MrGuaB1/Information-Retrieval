you ever stop to wonder where dirt comes from well an easy way to find out is to start a compost pile in your back yard composting is a process through which microorganisms break down organic matter kitchen vegetable scraps yard waste lawn clippings and convert it into soil through this cycle so called waste materials can become healthy nutrient rich soil perfect for replenishing our gardens or potted plants thankfully many simple and effective methods exist for composting on a small scale in your backyard or apartment contrary to some misperceptions composting does not have to be smelly or

your home's heating and cooling system is another simple and traditional approach to architecture you can use deciduous plants that have full green leaves in the summer and lose their leaves in the winter and plant them in front of south facing windows that need summer shading and winter sun exposure this can give you a beautifully natural canopy in the heat of june and july while letting those glorious rays shine in during december and january tips & tricks combine aesthetics and function choose vines or trees that will add to the beauty of your home learn about native deciduous plants in your area visit a local nursery of landscaping business and find out what recommendations they have web & print resources landscape shading www pioneerthinking com www eere energy gov find the solar

here's some tips choose a nutritious diet including an adventurous variety of fresh whole foods enjoy their color taste aroma and texture avoid processed foods enjoy reasonable exercise moving your body burns calories builds lean body mass and can reduce stress build self esteem and give you more energy eat slowly regularly and often studies show that people who eat three to five small meals a day successfully maintain a healthy weight eating breakfast can make you feel less hungry all foods can fit into a well balanced diet use the 80 20 rule if you eat well 80 of the time you can indulge

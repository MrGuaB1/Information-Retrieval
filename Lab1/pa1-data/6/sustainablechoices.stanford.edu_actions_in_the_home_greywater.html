precipitation and evaporation that carries water through our rivers lakes oceans and atmosphere gray water is defined as recycled water that has been used for some other purpose first namely in your shower sink or washing machine rather than using water once and then sending it down the drain it is possible to re use water for secondary applications such as toilet flushing or yard irrigation not

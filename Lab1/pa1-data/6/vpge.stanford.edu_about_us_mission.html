education vpge works across all seven schools at stanford university to enhance the quality of graduate education stanford is a highly decentralized university and responsibilities for graduate education lie primarily with its departments and schools vpge's mission is to assure that stanford remains at the forefront of graduate education providing the highest quality educational experiences for students our current priorities are to administer university wide graduate fellowship programs advance graduate student diversity foster cross school learning opportunities in interdisciplinary learning leadership and professional development coordinate and guide university wide graduate policy problem solve for university wide issues how we do this we actively collaborate with stanford's schools departments and central offices and with graduate student leaders and organizations our small staff works with others across campus on programs to advance university priorities the vpge provides resources and expertise convenes people and departments to discuss challenges in graduate education administers innovative projects in selected areas serves as a clearinghouse for information

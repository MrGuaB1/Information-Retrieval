simplicity carbon impact money savings health helper overview by making sure your home is well insulated you can improve your indoor climate control lower your energy bill and reduce greenhouse gas emissions a well insulated home can be much easier to keep warm in the winter and cool in the summer because less heat is transferred through the walls of the building in the winter you can invite the heat in during the day and then seal it in to warm you for the night in the summer you can open the windows at night and trap that cool air inside during the midday heat insulation is a no nonsense method of being energy wise tips & tricks look for recycled insulation materials some high performance insulation is made from recycled materials old newspapers or blue jeans consider a material that will insulate your home while creating a market for recycled products be safe and healthy be sure to do your homework on safety research the performance of insulation materials in fires as well as

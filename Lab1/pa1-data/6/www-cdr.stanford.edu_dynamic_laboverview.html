dynamic design lab lab overview dynamic design lab lab overview research directions people projects testbeds general information our research at the dynamic design laboratory centers on the generation and simplification of dynamic models for mechanical systems and the use of these models in the design of complex systems of particular interest are the integrated design and control of mechanical devices design for controllability and the application of advanced analysis and control techniques to ground vehicle design professor chris gerdes is our chief academic advisor and oversees all of our research he has worked with daimler benz in the vehicle systems technology center and currently teaches vehicle dynamics and control systems at stanford university we are located in the new mechanical engineering research lab room 130 near the center of the stanford university campus for a map of the campus click here or zoom in on our exact location comments and questions are welcome please call us at 1 650 724 4058 or send email to professor gerdes gerdes cdr stanford edu

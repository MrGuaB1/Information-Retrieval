report number cs tr 73 332 institution stanford university department of computer science title models of lcf author milner robin date january 1973 abstract lcf is a deductive system for computable functions proposed by d scott in 1969 in an unpublished memorandum the purpose of the present paper is to demonstrate the soundness of the system with respect to certain models which are partially ordered domains of continuous functions this demonstration was supplied by scott in his memorandum the present paper is merely intended to make this work more accessible ftp reports stanford edu pub cstr reports cs tr 73 332 cs tr 73 332 pdf

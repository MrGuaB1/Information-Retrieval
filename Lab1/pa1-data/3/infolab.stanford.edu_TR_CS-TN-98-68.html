report number cs tn 98 68 institution stanford university department of computer science title presenting html structure in audio user satisfaction with audio hypertext author james frankie date may 1998 abstract this paper discusses the results of a 2 by 4 mixed design experiment testing various ways of presenting html structures in audio four interface styles were tested 1 one speaker minimal sound effects 2 one speaker many sound effects 3 many speakers minimal sound effects and 4 many speakers many sound effects the results obtained were both specific to the interfaces used ie that the use of three different speakers to present heading levels was confusing and more general for example natural sounds are more distinguishable and easier to remember than tones a short discussion of typical html usage is also presented ftp reports stanford edu pub cstr reports cs tn 98 68 cs tn 98 68 pdf

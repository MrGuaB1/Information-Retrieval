sodium azide storage best storage conditions for sodium azide sodium azide should be stored in secondary containers in a cool dry secured storage it should not be stored in the same cabinet as acids heavy metals or their salts msds will have a longer list of specific incompatible materials location should be secure from heat shock or friction solutions of sodium azide do not pose the same danger of shock sensitivity associated with the solid form however the hydrazoic acid generated when sodium azide is dissolved is extremely toxic and explosive and should always be prepared inside a laboratory chemical hood

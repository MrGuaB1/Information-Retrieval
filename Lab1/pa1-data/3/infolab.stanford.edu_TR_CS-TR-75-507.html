report number cs tr 75 507 institution stanford university department of computer science title towards a semantic theory of dynamic binding author gordon michael jc date august 1975 abstract the results in this paper contribute to the formulation of a semantic theory of dynamic binding fluid variables the axioms and theorems are language independent in that they don t talk about programs ie syntactic objects but just about elements in certain domains firstly the equivalence in the circumstances where it's true of tying a knot through the environment elaborated in the paper and taking a least fixed point is shown this is central in proving the correctness of lisp eval type interpreters secondly the relation which must hold between two environments if a program is to have the same meaning in both is established it is shown how the theory can be applied to lisp to yield previously known facts ftp reports stanford edu pub cstr reports cs tr 75 507 cs tr 75 507 pdf

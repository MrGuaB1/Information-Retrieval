report number cs tr 69 121 institution stanford university department of computer science title accurate bounds for the eigenvalues of the laplacian and applications to rhombical domains author moler cleve b date february 1969 abstract we deal with the eigenvalues and eigenfunctions of laplace's differential operator on a bounded two dimensional domain g with zero values on the boundary the paper describes a new technique for determining the coefficients in the expansion of an eigenfunction in terms of particular eigenfunctions of the differential operator the coefficients are chosen to make the sum of the expansion come close to satisfying the boundary conditions as an example the eigenvalues and eigenfunctions are determined for a rhombical membrane ftp reports stanford edu pub cstr reports cs tr 69 121 cs tr 69 121 pdf

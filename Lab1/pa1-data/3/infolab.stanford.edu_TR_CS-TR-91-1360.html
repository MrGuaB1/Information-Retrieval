report number cs tr 91 1360 institution stanford university department of computer science title sooner is safer than later author henzinger thomas a date may 1991 abstract it has been repeatedly observed that the standard safety liveness classification of properties of reactive systems does not fit for real time properties this is because the implicit liveness of time shifts the spectrum towards the safety side while for example response that something good will happen eventually is a classical liveness property bounded response that something good will happen soon within a certain amount of time has many characteristics of safety we account for this phenomenon formally by defining safety and liveness relative to a given condition such as the progress of time ftp reports stanford edu pub cstr reports cs tr 91 1360 cs tr 91 1360 pdf

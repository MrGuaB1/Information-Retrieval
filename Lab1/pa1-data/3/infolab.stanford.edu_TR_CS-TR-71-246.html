report number cs tr 71 246 institution stanford university department of computer science title a resemblance test for the validation of a computer simulation of paranoid processes author colby kenneth mark author hilf franklin dennis author weber sylvia author kraemer helena c date november 1971 abstract a computer simulation of paranoid processes in the form of a dialogue algorithm was subjected to a validation study using an experimental resemblance test in which judges rated degrees of paranoia present in initial psychiatric interviews of both paranoid patients and of versions of the paranoid model the statistical results indicate a satisfactory degree of resemblance between the two groups of interviews it is concluded that the model provides a successful simulation of naturally occuring paranoid processes ftp reports stanford edu pub cstr reports cs tr 71 246 cs tr 71 246 pdf

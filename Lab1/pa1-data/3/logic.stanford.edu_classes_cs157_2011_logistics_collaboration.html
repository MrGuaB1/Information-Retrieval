cs157 information cs157 collaboration policy under the honor code at stanford each of you is expected to submit your own work in this course on many occasions however it is useful to work with other students or to ask for general advice from the course staff or other experts such activity is both acceptable and encouraged but you must indicate any collaboration or assistance on your solution sets any collaboration or assistance that is not given proper citation may be considered a violation of the honor code furthermore study groups should be limited to three students in any event you are responsible for understanding and being able to explain the solutions you submit the course staff will pursue aggressively any suspected cases of honor code violations c copyright 1995 2007 by michael genesereth

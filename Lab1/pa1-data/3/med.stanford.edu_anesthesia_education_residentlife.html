education and clinical training which results in the development of skilled consultant anesthesiologists and future leaders in the field our department is dedicated to providing a high quality of life for its residents through a variety of programs including work hour limitations an innovated comp time system that acknowledges and rewards resident work contributions and an annual department sponsored two day resident retreat at a resort in california annual resident retreat each spring residents attend a two day retreat in lake tahoe where they enjoy camaraderie amidst the beauty and tranquility of the largest fresh water lake in the sierra nevada mountains the department of anesthesia provides resort accommodations and recreational activities for residents so that they can have an opportunity to unwind from the rigors of residency training with their colleagues stanford anesthesia faculty provide full clinical coverage for residents during this retreat stanford life if you decide to come to stanford for your residency training you will live in one of the most beautiful and diverse areas in the world a typical weekend might include hiking in the marin headlands shopping for organic produce at a local farmer's market or dining in san francisco a city that is widely regarded as the nation's mecca for innovative cuisine closer to home stanford university offers a plethora of recreational activities the campus houses one million gross feet of indoor athletic facilities including four pools thirty tennis courts three fitness centers and a championship golf

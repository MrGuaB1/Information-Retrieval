history collections and services government information guides access & circulation the jonsson library is open to the public through any of the green library entrances the jonsson library stack collections are located in the bing wing us federal documents are on the basement and west 1 stack levels international british and california documents are located on the west 2 stack level circulation policies jonsson library materials circulate from the green library loan desk the automated circulation system requires presentation of a current valid borrower's card for all circulation transactions green library's circulation policies apply loan periods in general jonsson library materials circulate according to standard green library loan periods however the following materials are non circulating congressional record federal register congressional serial set california legislative journals british parliamentary papers and debates united states censuses materials must be returned to the green library loan desk or book return drop visitors individuals who wish to use materials in the jonsson library but who are not affiliated with stanford university or an institution with which it has formal cooperative arrangements should register at the privileges office during its regular business hours visitors should indicate that they wish to use government documents

report number cs tn 98 84 institution stanford university department of computer science title lessons from developing audio html interfaces author james frankie date july 1998 abstract in this paper we discuss our previous research on the establishment of guidelines and principles for choosing sounds to use in an audio interface to html called the aha framework these principles along with issues related to the target audience such as user tasks goals and interests are factors that can help us to choose specific sounds for the interface we conclude by describing scenarios of two potential users and the interfaces that would seem to be appropriate for them ftp reports stanford edu pub cstr reports cs tn 98 84 cs tn 98 84 pdf

christos faloutsos is a professor at carnegie mellon university he has received the presidential young investigator award by the national science foundation 1989 the research contributions award in icdm 2006 the sigkdd innovations award 2010 seventeen best paper awards and four teaching awards he has served as a member of the executive committee of sigkdd he has published over 200 refereed articles 11 book chapters and one monograph he holds five patents and he has given over 30 tutorials and over 10 invited distinguished lectures his research interests include data mining for graphs and streams fractals database performance and indexing for multimedia and bio informatics data

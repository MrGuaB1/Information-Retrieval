materials related to health care medical education and medical research at stanford and beyond we are especially interested in unpublished written documents such as correspondence administrative records and research materials and images generally we are not looking to collect journal reprints or other published materials unless those materials were published before 1900 or are otherwise very rare we do not offer to appraise the monetary value of rare books or other materials persons seeking appraisal services are encouraged to consult the listings of appraisers provided by the antiquarian

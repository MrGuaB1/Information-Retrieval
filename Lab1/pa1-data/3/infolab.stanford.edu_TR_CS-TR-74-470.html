report number cs tr 74 470 institution stanford university department of computer science title stable sorting and merging with optimal space and time bounds author trabb pardo luis i date december 1974 abstract this work introduces two algorithms for stable merging and stable sorting of files the algorithms have optimal worst case time bounds the merge is linear and the sort is of order n log n extra storage requirements are also optimal since both algorithms make use of a fixed number of pointers files are handled only by means of the primitives exchange and comparison of records and basic pointer transformations ftp reports stanford edu pub cstr reports cs tr 74 470 cs tr 74 470 pdf

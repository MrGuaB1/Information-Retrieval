regular instruction with continuing programmatic need in departments and programs persons of special expertise or scholarly distinction either in the local community or on the nonteaching university staff who are asked on an occasional basis to give a course or part of a course in their special fields individuals who are employed to meet specific departmental needs created by faculty leaves or unanticipated student load the title of senior lecturer is associated with a demonstrably higher level of responsibility and teaching excellence than that of a lecturer rather than with seniority or

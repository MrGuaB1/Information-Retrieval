in medhub may result in disciplinary action including suspension from the residency or termination from the residency program note that medhub locks duty hour entry at the end of each month residency coordinators can assist with back entry of hours with respect to duty hours both on site and off site all house staff must comply with the rules of the department to which they are assigned and must also comply with any applicable acgme state or federal regulations setting limitations on duty hours professional activities in your off time hours should be arranged so as not to interfere with your house officer obligations and your ability to benefit from your graduate medical education program please review the following documents and contact your program director if you have any questions

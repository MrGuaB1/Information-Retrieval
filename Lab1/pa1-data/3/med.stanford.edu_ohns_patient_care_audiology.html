licensed and certified healthcare professionals who measure and evaluate a person's ability to hear and specialize in the treatment of hearing disorders they also measure vestibular function as it relates to balance and balance disorders audiologists characterize the functional status of the auditory system and provide a variety of approaches to help counteract the effects of hearing losses including the provision of a wide variety of listening devices including hearing aids audiologists also characterize the functional status of the vestibular system and provide a variety of approaches

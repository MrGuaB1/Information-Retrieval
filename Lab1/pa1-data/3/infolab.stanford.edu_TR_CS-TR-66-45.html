report number cs tr 66 45 institution stanford university department of computer science title relaxation methods for semi definite systems author kahan william date august 1966 abstract certain non stationary relaxation iterations which are commonly applied to positive definite symmetric systems of linear equations are also applicable to a semi definite system provided that system is consistent some of the convergence theory of the former application is herein extended to the latter application the effects of rounding errors and of inconsistency are discussed too but with few helpful conclusions finally the application of these relaxation iterations to an indefinite system is shown here to be ill advised because these iterations will almost certainly diverge exponentially ftp reports stanford edu pub cstr reports cs tr 66 45 cs tr 66 45 pdf

to give find a person initiative to optimize educational effectiveness stanford otolaryngology is in the midst of a multi year project to enhance the effectiveness of our educational programs by encouraging active learning the central premise is to move from a culture of passive lectures to interactive learning sessions marked by a high degree of learner participation and vibrant discussion of issues we aspire to create a supportive intellectually robust environment in which learners are willing to take risks pose more questions and become engaged in scholarly dialog we strive to encourage the attitude that your critic is your best friend as open dialog regarding the strength of scientific arguments fosters excellence our grand rounds series now routinely incorporates audience response technology and many sessions are enriched with video 3d animations and immersive learning techniques residents are encouraged to participate in the creation of new technology based education resources through the virtual training facility in the department's perkins microsurgery laboratory as well as in the stanford center of simulated and immersive learning cisl http cisl stanford edu in

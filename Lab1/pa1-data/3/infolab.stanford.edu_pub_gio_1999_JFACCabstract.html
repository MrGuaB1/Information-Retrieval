jfacc abstract r simql an interface for integrating access to simulations into information systems gio wiederhold and hector garcia molina advances in enterprise control aec proc of the darpa jfacc symposium san diego ca nov 1999 pp 259 262 full paper we have performed proof of concept research on prediction software interfaces for information system such software can aid in the projection of effects of command and control c2 decisions and allows alternate courses of action coas to be evaluated our objective is to significantly augment command and control decision services by making predictive results of simulations and similar software as accessible as databases and other information components the motivating concept is that an interface language allows separation of customers and providers and that the autonomy created allows progress to be made independently

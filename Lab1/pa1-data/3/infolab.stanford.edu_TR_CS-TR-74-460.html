report number cs tr 74 460 institution stanford university department of computer science title random insertion into a priority queue structure author porter thomas author simon istvan date october 1974 abstract the average number of levels that a new element moves up when inserted into a heap is investigated two probabilistic models under which such an average might be computed are proposed a lemma of conservation of ignorance is formulated and used in the derivation of an exact formula for the average in one of these models it is shown that this average is bounded by a constant and its asymptotic behavior is discussed numerical data for the second model is also provided and analyzed ftp reports stanford edu pub cstr reports cs tr 74 460 cs tr 74 460 pdf

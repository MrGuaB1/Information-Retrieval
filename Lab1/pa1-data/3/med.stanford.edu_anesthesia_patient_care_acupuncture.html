lane library ways to give find a person medical acupuncture acupuncture is a several thousand year old discipline which originated in china and is now practiced throughout the world it is commonly used for the treatment of pain and alleviation of symptoms it involves the placement of very small needles in the body often in various locations on the arms and legs conditions for which acupuncture may be useful include post operative pain nausea and vomiting headache back pain and spasm dysmenorrhea pelvic pain infertility breech version hyperemesis of pregnancy cancer pain and others the

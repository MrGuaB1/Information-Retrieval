welcome stanford know more know your limits no abuse know more's goal is to help prevent teenage relationship abuse by giving informative and dynamic presentations at local high schools our skits and monologues serve to help students identify the signs of abusive relationships offer model healthy teenage relationships and guidance to appropriate resources for students in abusive relationships the hope is that our message will counter the abusive behavior that they may see at home in the media or elsewhere we hope to make a difference in the lives of the students we interact with

report number cs tr 87 1144 institution stanford university department of computer science title considerations for multiprocessor typologies author byrd gregory author delagi bruce date january 1987 abstract choosing a multiprocessor interconnection topology may depend on high level considerations such as the intended application domain and the expected number of processors it certainly depends on low level implementation details such as packaging and communications protocols we first use rough measures of cost and performance to characterize several topologies we then examine how implementation details can affect the realizable performance of a topology ftp reports stanford edu pub cstr reports cs tr 87 1144 cs tr 87 1144 pdf

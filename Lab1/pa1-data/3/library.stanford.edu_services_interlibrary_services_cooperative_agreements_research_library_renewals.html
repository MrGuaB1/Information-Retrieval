through the rlcp program may usually be renewed at least once each institution has slightly different procedures please refer to the appropriate section below for specific instructions books borrowed from uc berkeley when a patron renews for the first time via uc berkley's oskicat the patron is required to create a pin number 1 click on the my oskicat link in the upper right corner of the ucb catalogue 2 enter patron name stanford library id and the generic pin number 3403 alternately you may use your stanford id instead of library id but preface it with the capitalized letters su for example su01010203 3 click on the modify pin button to customize the pin number 4 my oskicat will display the items that are checked out 5 to renew all materials at once press the renew all button 6 to renew selected material check the box beside the title in the renew column 7 click the renew selected button when items are renewed successfully a new due date will appear in the status column patrons who experience difficulty customizing their pin number may call uc berkeley rlcp for assistance 510 642 2538 renewals may also be requested by sending an e mail to rlcp library berkeley edu with a list of the books you want to renew books borrowed from stanford send an e mail to cooperative stanford edu list each book by author title and barcode number you will be notified of the new due dates books borrowed from ut austin send an e mail to rlcp lib utexas edu list each book by author title and barcode number you will be notified of the new due dates last modified december 30 2011

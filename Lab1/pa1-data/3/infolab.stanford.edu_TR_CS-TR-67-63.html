report number cs tr 67 63 institution stanford university department of computer science title graeffe's method for eigenvalues author polya george date april 1967 abstract let an entire function f z of finite genus have infinitely many zeros which are all positive and take real values for real z then it is shown how to give two sided bounds for all the zeros of f in terms of the coefficients of the power series of f and of coefficients obtained by graeffe's algorithm applied to f a simple numerical illustration is given for a bessel function ftp reports stanford edu pub cstr reports cs tr 67 63 cs tr 67 63 pdf

improved pedestrian crossing to be constructed across campus drive news release 04 13 92 contact stanford university news service 415 723 2558 improved pedestrian crossing to be constructed across campus drive stanford work will begin thursday april 23 on the construction of an improved crosswalk across campus drive between parking structure one and the medical center the crosswalk will be made of decorative pavers and elevated about three inches providing a speed hump that should slow traffic on campus drive officials with facilities project management say in conjunction with the crosswalk work the medical center side barrel of campus drive between quarry road and roth way also will be reconstructed to allow for the two projects that barrel of campus drive will be closed for a three week period starting april 23 during the closure campus drive through traffic will be detoured via lomita drive and roth way the main campus side barrel of campus drive will be closed one lane at a time and will not require a detour pedestrians crossing between the parking structure and medical center will be detoured to the crossing at roth way the work will be completed prior to commencement z 920413arc2297 html this is an archived release this release is not available in any other form images mentioned in this release are not available online stanford news service has an extensive library of images some of which may be available to you online direct your request by email to images news service stanford edu stanford university all rights reserved stanford ca 94305 650 723 2300 terms of use copyright complaints

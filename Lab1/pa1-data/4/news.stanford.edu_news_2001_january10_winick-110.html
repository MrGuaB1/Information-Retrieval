winick receives distinguished associate award from department of energy 1 01 stanford report online stanford report january 10 2001 winick receives distinguished associate award from department of energy herman winick physics professor at the stanford synchrotron radiation laboratory recently received the distinguished associate award from the department of energy doe this is the doe's highest award and is given only to those who have made significant accomplishments in their fields the citation commends winick for his contributions and leadership in the development of modern synchrotron light sources especially in promoting this technology across disciplinary and geographical boundaries

celebration for renovated art gallery mfa exhibition may 17 5 01 stanford report online stanford report may 16 2001 celebration for renovated art gallery mfa exhibition may 17 to celebrate the re opening of the thomas welton stanford art gallery as well as the opening of the 2001 master of fine arts exhibition a reception is scheduled from 6 to 9 pm thursday in the art gallery provost john etchemendy is scheduled to speak and there will be refreshments the event is free and open to the public the building was renovated and retrofitted over an 11 month period that began in june 2000

panofsky receives international scientific and technological cooperation award 2 01 stanford report online stanford report february 28 2001 panofsky receives international scientific and technological cooperation award wolfgang kh panofsky professor emeritus and former director of the stanford linear accelerator center has been awarded the international scientific and technological cooperation award from the people's republic of china for his contributions to the development of science and technology in that country the award was established in 1993 to acknowledge the contributions of foreign scientists

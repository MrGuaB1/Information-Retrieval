annual zoned flushing of campus water system to begin skip to content stanford university stanford news submit home all news faculty & staff news for journalists about us print stanford report february 11 2009 annual zoned flushing of campus water system to begin produced by maps and records the stanford water department will conduct its annual flushing of the university's water distribution system from feb 17 through march 6 during that time fire hydrants will be opened to flush out sediment that has collected in the mains during the past year the flushing a standard operating procedure for water departments may cause slight but temporary reduction in water pressure and possible discoloration however the department assures that the water will remain safe for drinking the department also suggests users refrain from doing projects that may be affected by temporary sediments in the water while the lines are being flushed the schedule for flushing follows see map zone 1 residential area south of campus drive east and mayfield avenue starts tuesday feb 17 and ends thursday feb 19 nightly from 7 to 11 pm zone 2 central campus and academic areas approximately within campus drive loop additionally north to sand hill road and between bowdoin street and mayfield avenue starts monday feb 23 and ends friday feb 27 nightly from 7 to 11 pm zone 3 area east of campus drive east north of bowdoin street and east of serra street boundary starts wednesday march 4 and ends friday march 6 daily from 10 am to 3 pm for additional information call the water department at 723 1300 or utilities at 723 9747 or visit http lbre stanford edu sem environmental share this story related to this story environmental quality and compliance contact directories maps & directions stanford university all rights reserved stanford ca 94305 650 723 2300

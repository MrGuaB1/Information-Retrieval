stanford native american cultural center nacc trailer welcoming everyone to our new home about the center the native american cultural center at stanford is often described as the heart of our community and as a key to interactive learning the center serves the community as a resource center gathering place student organization headquarters for over twenty groups study hall tutoring center computer center classroom and the location of frequent social cultural and educational programs it is a place to meet friends a comfortable stop between classes a home away from home and a place to receive moral support students have 24 hour access to the center which comes in very handy during final exam week the center is located at the heart of campus close to muwekma tah ruk up the road from the black community services center across the way from el centro chicano and downstairs from the asian american activities center special note during the 2006 academic year the native american cultural center will be relocated click the picture above or here to learn more back

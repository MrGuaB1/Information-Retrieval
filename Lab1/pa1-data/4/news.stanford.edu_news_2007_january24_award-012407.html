nominations sought for deans award skip to content stanford university stanford news submit home all news faculty & staff news for journalists about us print stanford report january 24 2007 nominations sought for deans award faculty members are encouraged to nominate candidates for the deans award for academic accomplishment the deadline for nominations is friday feb 23 inaugurated in the spring of 1988 the awards honor five to 10 undergraduates for extraordinary academic accomplishments that might not otherwise be celebrated examples include exceptional tangible achievements in classes or independent research national academic competition a presentation or publication for a regional or national audience or superior performance in the creative arts among the recipients of last year's awards were victoria parikh a senior who was honored for her work on the role of endocrinological responses in behavioral coping strategies and jeffrey trevino a senior who was honored for his work in composition performance and musicology as well as his commitment to the stanford student arts community award winners are presented with a specially selected book and citation at a spring quarter ceremony their portraits and citations will be displayed in the first floor lobby of sweet hall and at the undergraduate research program's website http urp stanford edu click on the awards link to see photographs and citations of previous deans award winners letters of recommendation should be sent to the office of undergraduate advising and research 126a sweet hall mail code 3085 or via e mail to urp vpue stanford edu share this story contact directories maps & directions stanford university all rights reserved stanford ca 94305 650 723 2300

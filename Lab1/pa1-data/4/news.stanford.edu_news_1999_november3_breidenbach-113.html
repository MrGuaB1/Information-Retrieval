marty breidenbach receives panofsky prize stanford university home stanford news archive stanford report november 3 1999 marty breidenbach receives panofsky prize marty breidenbach professor at the stanford linear accelerator center recently received the wkh panofsky prize in experimental particle physics he was cited for his many contributions to e e physics especially with the sld detector at the stanford linear collider that have contributed to advances in the measurement of electroweak parameters and in accelerator technology the $5000 award is presented annually in recognition of outstanding achievement in experimental particle physics and was established in 1985 by friends of panofsky slac director emeritus stanford news stanford homepage stanford university all rights reserved stanford ca 94305 650 723 2300

2012 voting under way for next year's faculty senate approximately half of the 55 elected members of the senate are up for re election voting has begun to elect members of the 45th senate of the academic council and to nominate full professors to fill upcoming vacancies on the academic council's advisory board the voting which began at midnight feb 21 ends at 11 59 pm march 10 for the sixth year in a row the election will be conducted online allowing voters to cast ballots from their computers on a secure webpage all members of the academic council will receive emails advising them of the election and providing the link for submitting votes last year about 50 percent of the members of the academic council cast votes voters will be required to sign in with their sunet ids to cast ballots and can do so from any web enabled computer approximately half of the 55 elected members of the senate are up for re election members of the senate serve two year staggered terms faculty members also will be nominating full professors to fill two upcoming vacancies on the seven member advisory board of the academic council the two electoral groups are electoral group i gsb school of education and school of law and electoral group iii school of humanities and sciences section a natural sciences the second round of voting for the 2012 13 advisory board which will be open to all members of the academic council will be held in april the results of that election will be announced during spring quarter elections for the chair of the senate and for its steering committee will be held in april the results of the election will be announced in spring quarter the senate which was established in 1968 also includes 15 ex officio members president john hennessy provost john etchemendy the deans of the seven schools the directors of the hoover institution and the slac national accelerator laboratory the vice provosts for undergraduate and for graduate education the dean

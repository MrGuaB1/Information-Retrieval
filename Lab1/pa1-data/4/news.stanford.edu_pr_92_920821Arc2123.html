stanford voice mail unavailable this weekend news release 08 21 92 contact stanford university news service 415 723 2558 stanford voice mail unavailable this weekend stanford stanford will be without voice mail services the weekend of aug 29 30 while communication services upgrades the system during this period which extends from 12 01 am saturday aug 29 to 5 am monday aug 31 callers cannot leave voice mail messages and users cannot retrieve messages in their mailboxes according to jan thomson director for communication services the change will allow stanford to meet the increasing demand for voice mailboxes as well as enhance existing voice mail features voice mailboxes will work the same on the new system as they do on the current system the voice mail system telephone number 3 0000 and mailbox numbers passwords and messages will remain the same communication services is requesting that users remove any unnecessary saved messages from their mailboxes prior to aug 29 this will help the department speed up the conversion portion of the upgrade communication services has sent department stars stanford telecommunication activity representatives information describing the change for members of their departments for more information employees should contact their de partment star or call communication services at 5 help 920821arc2123 html this is an archived release this release is not available in any other form images mentioned in this release are not available online stanford news service has an extensive library of images some of which may be available to you online direct your request by email to images news service stanford edu stanford university all rights reserved stanford ca 94305 650 723 2300 terms of use copyright complaints

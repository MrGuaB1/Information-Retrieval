schawlow to receive american innovator award 9 96 news release 9 23 96 contact stanford university news service 415 723 2558 schawlow to receive american innovator award stanford arthur l schawlow professor emeritus of physics and co inventor of the laser has been selected to receive the ronald h brown american innovator award the award given by the us department of commerce was established last year to honor the outstanding contributions made to society by us inventors it is named for the secretary of commerce who was died in an airplane crash in april during a trade mission to bosnia the award will be presented in washington dc on oct 15 in the lincoln gallery of the national museum of american art nobel laureate schawlow was honored for his contributions to the development of the laser which has become an invaluable tool in communications industry medicine military operations and scientific research dfs 96023schawlow html download this release and its related files the release is provided in adobe acrobat format any images shown in the release are provided at publishing quality additional images also may be provided complete credit and caption information is included 96023schawlow sit stanford university all rights reserved stanford ca 94305 650 723 2300 terms of use copyright complaints stanford university all rights reserved stanford ca 94305 650 723 2300 terms of use copyright complaints

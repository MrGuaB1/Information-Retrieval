chorus is a non auditioned community chorus sponsored by the stanford department of music membership is open to everyone singers typically come from all corners of the bay area and from all age groups and ability levels concerts this year the summer chorus will be presenting a program of early american choral music featuring works from the california missions baroque style works from the moravian church in pennsylvania and north carolina anglo american hymns anthems and fuguing tunes african american work songs and spirituals and popular american folk songs our concert in memorial church on saturday august 6th at 8 pm will present a fascinating slice of american history showcasing the incredible variety and quality of american choral music from colonial times to the 20th century

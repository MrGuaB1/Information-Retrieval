simonson receives 2001 o dell award from the american marketing association 5 01 stanford report online stanford report may 30 2001 simonson receives 2001 o dell award from the american marketing association itamar simonson the sebastian s kresge professor of marketing at the graduate school of business received the 2001 o dell award from the american marketing association for a research article judged to have had the greatest impact on the field of marketing during the five years following its publication the effect of new product features on brand choice coauthored with his former student steve nowlis now associate professor of marketing at arizona state university was published in 1996 in the journal of marketing research this is simonson's second o dell award

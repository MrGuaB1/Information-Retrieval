hagstrom receives john ericsson gold medal for contributions to higher education stanford university home stanford news archive stanford report may 24 2000 hagstrom receives john ericsson gold medal for contributions to higher education stig b hagstrom professor emeritus of materials science and engineering received the john ericsson gold medal of the american association of swedish engineers for his contributions to higher education both in the united states and in sweden hagstrom recently returned to stanford after a six year term as chancellor of the swedish university system he was also made an honorary member of the royal academy of engineering sciences iva in sweden for exceptionally valuable contributions to the academy a recognition held by only nine of the academy members he served as chairman of the academy from 1993 to 1996 stanford news stanford homepage stanford university all rights reserved stanford ca 94305 650 723 2300

administrative action taken against three who face drug charges 7 01 stanford report online stanford report july 25 2001 administrative action taken against three who face drug charges stanford university has taken administrative action against two recent graduates murat goksel 23 and berra yazar 22 and sophomore christopher pumo 20 which effectively bars them from campus until further notice the three were arrested on friday june 29 in escondido village on numerous felony charges including conspiracy and possession for sale of certain controlled substances the matter also has been forwarded to the judicial affairs office for investigation as a possible violation of the university's fundamental standard the arrests were the result of a months long undercover investigation by the palo alto police department and a santa clara county special enforcement team into illegal drug activities in cooperation with stanford's department of public safety

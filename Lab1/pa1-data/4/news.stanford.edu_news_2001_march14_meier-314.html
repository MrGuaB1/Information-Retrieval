meier named an honorary professor at peruvian university 3 01 stanford report online stanford report march 14 2001 meier named an honorary professor at peruvian university gerald meier the konosuke matsushita professor of international economics and policy analysis emeritus was named an honorary professor at the graduate school of management escuela de administracion de negocios para graduado in lima peru where he gave the 2000 commencement address in december in his address meier noted that the stanford graduate school of business had been instrumental in creating its peruvian counterpart and that former dean ernest arbuckle spoke at the school's dedication in 1964 meier is coeditor and contributing author of a new book the frontiers of development economics published jointly by oxford university press and the world bank

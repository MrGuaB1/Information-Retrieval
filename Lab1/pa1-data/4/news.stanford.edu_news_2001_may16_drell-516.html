drell honored for contributions to national security 5 01 stanford report online stanford report may 16 2001 drell honored for contributions to national security sidney drell professor emeritus at the stanford linear accelerator center and senior fellow at the hoover institution was awarded this year's william o baker award at a may 17 ceremony at the defense intelligence center of bolling air force base in washington dc the award is bestowed on us citizens who have made lifelong contributions to national security particularly in the field of foreign intelligence

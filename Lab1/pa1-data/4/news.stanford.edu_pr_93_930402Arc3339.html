margaret jenkins dance company to give demonstration news release 04 02 93 contact stanford university news service 415 723 2558 margaret jenkins dance company to give demonstration stanford the margaret jenkins dance company will present a free lecture demonstration at 7 30 pm friday april 16 in the roble dance studio the event is sponsored by the stanford dance division jenkins and her seven member san francisco based company will present excerpts from breathe normally a jenkins work in progress that will have its world premiere in july in chicago breathe normally which will be an evening length work when completed is being made as part of the celebrations for the company's 20th anniversary session it features an original language score created jointly by poet michael palmer and singer composer rinde eckert after an hour long demonstration jenkins and her company will be joined by palmer and stanford english professor marjorie perloff an authority on the group of language poets of which palmer is a member for a half hour question and answer session with the audience the jenkins company founded in 1973 is among the major touring contemporary dance ensembles in the united states since its founding the company has premiered and performed more than 60 of jenkins works in recent years the company has toured the soviet union england france belgium and singapore mas lid jenkins 930402arc3339 html this is an archived release this release is not available in any other form images mentioned in this release are not available online stanford news service has an extensive library of images some of which may be available to you online direct your request by email to images news service stanford edu stanford university all rights reserved stanford ca 94305 650 723 2300 terms of use copyright complaints

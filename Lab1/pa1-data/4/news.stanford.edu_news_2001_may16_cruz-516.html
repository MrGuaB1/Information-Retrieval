nadinne cruz honored by u of massachusetts 5 01 stanford report online stanford report may 16 2001 nadinne cruz honored by u of massachusetts nadinne cruz director of the haas center for public service lecturer in urban studies and resident fellow at okada house recently received the distinguished citizen scholar award from the university of massachusetts the annual award presented by commonwealth college the honors college at the university of massachusetts recognizes an individual who has an outstanding record of translating civic ideals into action

editor's advisory revised sand hill road stanford shopping center and stanford west proposals to be presented at april 28 community meeting news release 04 26 94 contact stanford university news service 415 723 2558 editor's advisory revised sand hill road stanford shopping center and stanford west proposals to be presented at april 28 community meeting stanford stanford university officials will present revised proposals to improve sand hill road expand the stanford shopping center and construct multi family and senior housing communities on the site known as stanford west at a community meeting thursday april 28 the meeting will begin at 7 pm at the first congregational church 1985 louis road at embarcadero in palo alto stanford's original proposals have been significantly revised as a result of community comment received during a just completed seven month community outreach program more than 800 residents of menlo park and palo alto participated in the program through attendance at small neighborhood and larger community meetings as well as workshop sessions after the april 28 meeting at which details of the new stanford proposals will be unveiled stanford then will submit the proposals to the cities of palo alto and menlo park palo alto as the lead public agency will conduct the environmental impact review process pr 940426arc4312 html this is an archived release this release is not available in any other form images mentioned in this release are not available online stanford news service has an extensive library of images some of which may be available to you online direct your request by email to images news service stanford edu stanford university all rights reserved stanford ca 94305 650 723 2300 terms of use copyright complaints

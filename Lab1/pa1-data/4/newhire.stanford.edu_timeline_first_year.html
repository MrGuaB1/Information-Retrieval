energy retirement savings plan now available celebrate your one year anniversary at stanford by enrolling in the stanford contributory retirement plan scrp the scrp is a flexible and robust savings tool that allows you to contribute a percentage of your salary on either a before tax or after tax basis plus whether or not you enroll stanford rewards you with a basic contribution based on your salary and years of service if you enroll and make contributions of your own you become eligible for matching contributions from stanford over time your contributions plus stanford's basic and matching contributions add up to a powerful savings tool to learn more about scrp attend a one year anniversary meeting by visiting the benefits events & workshops page in addition review all your retirement savings options at the benefits website staff tuition reimbursement program for degree pursuits stanford is all about educating tomorrow's leaders and that includes you the staff tuition reimbursement program strp supports staff academic development by providing partial or full reimbursement of tuition costs up to the internal revenue services maximum allowable per year for courses meeting requirements of undergraduate or graduate degree programs staff members must be enrolled in a degree program at an accredited college or university to qualify for assistance and must have completed one year in an ongoing regular position benefits are pro rated for regular staff working less than full time for full information please visit the human resources educational assistance programs website recommended questions to ask at this point in your stanford career you may be quite focused on the road ahead typically you may wish to address these issues with your immediate supervisor or manager how you can continue to be fully productive or possibly increase your effectiveness or job satisfaction in some way if you haven t already tell your manager what you love about your work and what you wish could be made better how you prefer to be recognized for your accomplishments identify any recurring hindrances to your productivity or effectiveness and ask for support to minimize or remove those hindrances when will i receive a salary increase how will i be informed of an increase talk about career development opportunities including if you want to participate in or lead a committee or initiative is there another position in the group department to which you aspire what training or knowledge does your manager recommend you complete or acquire to help you be

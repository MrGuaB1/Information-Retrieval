new definition for capital equipment 9 8 98 stanford university home stanford news archive stanford report september 9 1998 new definition for capital equipment 9 8 98 new definition for capital equipment in conjunction with the start of a new fiscal year university property administrators and the controller's office have changed the definition of capital equipment to mean as follows to be considered capital equipment all three of the following criteria must be met equipment has a useful life of more than one year stands alone meaning the property is not permanently attached to or integrated into a building or structure and has an acquisition cost of $1500 or more if the item will not have a useful life of more than one year in general it is considered expendable material even if it costs more than $1500 lisa forgatsch government cost and rate studies sr stanford news stanford homepage stanford university all rights reserved stanford ca 94305 650 723 2300

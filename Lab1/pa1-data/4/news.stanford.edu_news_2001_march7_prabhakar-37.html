shyam prabhakar receives award for outstanding doctoral thesis research in beam physics 3 01 stanford report online stanford report march 7 2001 shyam prabhakar receives award for outstanding doctoral thesis research in beam physics shyam prabhakar postdoctoral fellow in mathematics received the american physical society's 2001 award for outstanding doctoral thesis research in beam physics the citation recognizes prabhakar whose thesis adviser was consulting professor john fox of the stanford linear accelerator center and the applied physics department for development of beam instability formalisms and diagnostics based on transient domain beam measurements

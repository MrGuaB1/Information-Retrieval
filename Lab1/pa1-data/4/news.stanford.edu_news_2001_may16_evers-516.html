evers appointed to bush's commission on presidential scholars 5 01 stanford report online stanford report may 16 2001 evers appointed to bush's commission on presidential scholars williamson m evers a research fellow at the hoover institution recently was appointed to the commission on presidential scholars by president george w bush the 24 member commission selects and honors top achieving seniors in american high schools evers is a member of hoover's koret task force on k 12 education and was an education policy adviser to bush during his presidential campaign

water flushing set for feb 26 april 7 02 02 stanford university home stanford news archive stanford report february 13 2002 water flushing set for feb 26 april 7 the stanford water department will flush the stanford water distribution system starting feb 26 and ending april 7 the water department will open fire hydrants to flush out sediment that has collected in the mains during the past year this flushing program is a standard operating procedure for water departments flushing the mains may cause some slight but temporary reduction in water pressure and possible water discoloration however your water remains safe for drinking the utilities division suggests that you refrain from doing projects that may be affected by temporary impurities in the water while the water lines are being flushed in your particular area the flushing schedule follows zone 1 residential area south of campus drive east and mayfield avenue starts tuesday feb 26 and ends thursday feb 28 nightly from 7 to 11 pm zone 2 central campus and academic areas approximately within campus drive campus drive east and campus drive west boundaries starts tuesday march 5 and ends friday march 8 nightly from 7 to 11 pm zone 3 area east of campus drive east within bowdoin street and serra street boundaries starts saturday april 6 and ends sunday april 7 daily from 9 am to 3 pm for more information call the water department at 723 1300 or utilities at 725 7864 or visit www stanford edu group water water resources & environmental quality home page stanford news stanford homepage stanford university all rights reserved stanford ca 94305 650 723 2300

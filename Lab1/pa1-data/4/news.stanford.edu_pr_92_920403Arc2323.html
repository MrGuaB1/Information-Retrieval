stanford community carnival set for april 11 news release 04 03 92 contact stanford university news service 415 723 2558 stanford community carnival set for april 11 stanford the 1992 stanford community carnival a benefit for the arbor free clinic is scheduled for 10 am to 4 pm saturday april 11 at florence moore field on mayfield avenue in the central campus the carnival is sponsored by the stanford volunteer network and the haas center for public service events include game booths an improvisational theater group a dunking booth musical and dance entertainment and food and drinks children receive 12 free tickets to use for booths and food adults and stanford students are charged 25 cents per ticket as are children who want to use more than a dozen tickets proceeds go to the new arbor free clinic which offers free medical care by students at the stanford school of medicine to the homeless and other needy people for more information contact the student volunteer network at 415 723 0054 the coordinator of the 1992 carnival is elson liu pr 920403arc2323 html this is an archived release this release is not available in any other form images mentioned in this release are not available online stanford news service has an extensive library of images some of which may be available to you online direct your request by email to images news service stanford edu stanford university all rights reserved stanford ca 94305 650 723 2300 terms of use copyright complaints

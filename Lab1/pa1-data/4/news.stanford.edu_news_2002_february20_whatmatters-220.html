ellen waxman to speak in what matters series 02 02 stanford university home stanford news archive stanford report february 20 2002 ellen waxman to speak in what matters series ellen waxman university ombuds and director of the stanford mediation center will be the speaker feb 27 in the what matters to me and why series waxman is an attorney and pioneer in the work of ombuds a swedish word referring to an impartial dispute resolver she previously was the ombuds at the stanford medical school her talk will begin at noon in the side chapel of memorial church the purpose of what matters to me and why is to encourage reflection within the stanford community on matters of personal values beliefs and motivations in order to better understand the lives and inspirations of those who shape the university for more information or to plan upcoming speakers contact lisa mendelman at lisame stanford edu the series is supported by the deans for religious life and the associated students ombuds office and mediation center stanford news stanford homepage stanford university all rights reserved stanford ca 94305 650 723 2300

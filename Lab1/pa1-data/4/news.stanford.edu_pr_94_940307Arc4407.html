cox medal nominations solicited news release 03 07 94 contact stanford university news service 415 723 2558 cox medal nominations solicited stanford the subcommittee on university and departmental honors of the committee on academic appraisal and achievement c aaa invites members of the stanford community to nominate faculty for the allan cox medal for faculty excellence fostering undergraduate research the cox medal is awarded annually to a member of the stanford faculty who has established a record of excellence directing undergraduate research in addition to the bronze medal traditionally presented to the faculty recipient this year's winner will receive a $1500 cash prize made possible by a gift from the amoco foundation the award was instituted in memory of allan cox who was a professor of geophysics and dean of the school of earth sciences co discoverer of magnetic reversals cox was a strong advocate of student participation in scholarship letters of nomination should be sent to laura selznick undergraduate research opportunities 122 sweet hall mc 3088 or via e mail to laura selznick forsythe by friday may 6 the medal will be presented at the firestone golden medal award ceremony on saturday june 11 dmh 940307arc4407 html this is an archived release this release is not available in any other form images mentioned in this release are not available online stanford news service has an extensive library of images some of which may be available to you online direct your request by email to images news service stanford edu stanford university all rights reserved stanford ca 94305 650 723 2300 terms of use copyright complaints

mud volleyball to benefit muscular dystrophy association news release 01 07 93 contact stanford university news service 415 723 2558 mud volleyball to benefit muscular dystrophy association stanford stanford's kappa alpha fraternity will host its seventh annual mud volleyball classic from 9 am to 4 pm saturday jan 23 on the stanford campus the tournament played in six inches of mud on the lagunita lakebed has become one of the most visible fundraisers on campus in recent years proceeds collected from advertising sponsorships and team fees are donated to the muscular dystrophy association last year the event earned $10000 nearly 70 student groups businesses and clubs from throughout the bay area have entered teams and will bring along their own cheering sections the event is free and open to the public there will be food drink and music more information is available from christopher bulger at the kappa alpha house 664 lomita court stanford ca 94305 the phone number is 415 497 5405 tmj 930107arc3452 html this is an archived release this release is not available in any other form images mentioned in this release are not available online stanford news service has an extensive library of images some of which may be available to you online direct your request by email to images news service stanford edu stanford university all rights reserved stanford ca 94305 650 723 2300 terms of use copyright complaints

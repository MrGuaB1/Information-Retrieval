moffatt hancock to be remembered at jan 28 service news release 01 12 94 contact stanford university news service 415 723 2558 moffatt hancock to be remembered at jan 28 service stanford moffatt hancock the stanford law school's marion rice kirkwood professor emeritus will be remembered at a public service at 4 pm friday jan 28 in stanford memorial church an active member of the law school faculty for 23 years hancock died dec 5 at the stanford university medical center he is survived by his wife of 44 years eileen who lives on the stanford campus and two children and six grandchildren the jan 28 service will include tributes by law professor gerald gunther former professor john richard mcdonough judge james ware jd 1972 and peter koerber hancock's son in law following the memorial service at approximately 5 pm a reception will be held in the manning faculty lounge at the stanford law school for more information contact olivia styles at 415 723 4749 ad 940112arc4497 html this is an archived release this release is not available in any other form images mentioned in this release are not available online stanford news service has an extensive library of images some of which may be available to you online direct your request by email to images news service stanford edu stanford university all rights reserved stanford ca 94305 650 723 2300 terms of use copyright complaints

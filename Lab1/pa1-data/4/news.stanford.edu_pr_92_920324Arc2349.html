spring clean up for campus residents april 11 news release 03 24 92 contact stanford university news service 415 723 2558 spring clean up for campus residents april 11 peninsula sanitary service inc the campus primary garbage collector and recycling contractor has scheduled a spring clean up for saturday april 11 starting at 7 am the special collection offers residents of the faculty staff housing areas the opportunity to discard bagged boxed and bundled trash as well as furniture and appliances yard waste also will be collected but must be prepared and left apart from other materials accepted items include bagged trash less than 30 pounds per bag boxed trash less than 30 pounds per box bundled trash less than 30 pounds per bundle large branches less than four feet long furniture and appliances doors should be removed items that should not be included for curbside pickup are yard waste hazardous materials rocks dirt concrete or automobile parts yard waste should be placed 12 inches from the curb in compact piles no more than five feet high or wide this material must not be bagged residents who need more information on how to get rid of trash and other items during this special clean up should call peninsula sanitary service at 321 4236 pr 920324arc2349 html this is an archived release this release is not available in any other form images mentioned in this release are not available online stanford news service has an extensive library of images some of which may be available to you online direct your request by email to images news service stanford edu stanford university all rights reserved stanford ca 94305 650 723 2300 terms of use copyright complaints

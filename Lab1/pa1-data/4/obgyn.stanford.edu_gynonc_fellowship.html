year fully accredited program with emphasis on both clinical care and academic research in gynecologic cancer the first year of the fellowship is dedicated solely to basic science research at either institution based on the candidate's research interests the 2nd and 3rd year are clinical rotations on the gynecologic oncology service with 6 months spent in a continuous manner at each institution on a rotating basis there is one month of elective time each year and during the first year this is usually spent on an icu rotation application process applicants should submit online applications through eras and submit our supplemental application by email our program and our candidates will be matched through the nrmp system interviews will be held in the summer of 2012 by invitation our application deadline for both our supplemental application and the eras application is wednesday may 30 2012 click here to download the 2012 application for our program starting in july 2013 if you

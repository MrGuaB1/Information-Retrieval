vaden to offer flu shots for faculty staff through dec 6 skip to content stanford university stanford news submit home all news faculty & staff news for journalists about us print stanford report october 26 2005 vaden to offer flu shots for faculty staff through dec 6 vaden health center will hold on site flu immunization clinics on tuesdays from 3 to 6 pm through dec 6 with the exception of tuesday nov 22 when the clinic will close during the week of the thanksgiving holiday flu shots are free for university employees which includes faculty staff faculty emeriti retired staff and medical school and research faculty and staff university employees must present their stanford id card in order to be immunized the vaccine also is free for medical students with a student id the vaccine costs $22 for spouses and domestic partners of university employees as well as for students and their spouses or domestic partners students enrolled in cardinal care pay $11 the clinic is not serving children and other family members of university employees vaden first opened the clinic on oct 18 to individuals who fell into a high risk category either because of age chronic illness pregnancy or certain line of work vaden is not giving the vaccine to anyone with a severe cold or other infection or to those who have an allergy to eggs the health center is at 866 campus drive east for more information call 498 2336 or go to http vaden stanford edu share this story related to this story vaden health center contact directories maps & directions stanford university all rights reserved stanford ca 94305 650 723 2300

jazz workshop celebrates dizzy gillespie's 75th news release 07 27 92 contact stanford university news service 415 723 2558 jazz workshop celebrates dizzy gillespie's 75th stanford the stanford jazz workshop will honor jazz great dizzy gillespie's 75th birthday by presenting gillespie's diamond jubilee at 2 pm on saturday aug 15 the event will be held in frost amphitheater tickets are $20 for the lawn $24 for chair seating gates open at 12 30 more than 25 musicians from jazz legends to young masters are scheduled to appear they include milt jackson freddie hubbard james moody hank jones red rodney jimmy heath sweets edison giovanni hidalgo ignacio berroa steve turre and danilo perez the program highlighting gillespie's works will showcase a number of musical combinations including the bebop all stars the latin all stars and the united nations orchestra due to recent surgery gillespie is not expected to attend the event for more information or to purchase tickets call 415 725 arts or a bass ticket agency pr 920727arc2131 html this is an archived release this release is not available in any other form images mentioned in this release are not available online stanford news service has an extensive library of images some of which may be available to you online direct your request by email to images news service stanford edu stanford university all rights reserved stanford ca 94305 650 723 2300 terms of use copyright complaints

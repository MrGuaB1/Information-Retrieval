multimedia networking group stanford university the multimedia networking group led by professor fouad tobagi is part of the computer systems laboratory in the departments of computer science and electrical engineering at stanford university our research focuses on the assessment and resource provisioning for various types of traffic voice video data and multimedia over wired wireless and optical networks we aim at providing acceptable quality to the end user operating in the above listed networks there are 9 graduate students and 3 visting scholars working in our group on different research topics group members in front of packard building group links stanford university created by jiang zhu and lola awoniyi last updated 10 28 02 contact jiangzhu stanford edu for web site suggestions

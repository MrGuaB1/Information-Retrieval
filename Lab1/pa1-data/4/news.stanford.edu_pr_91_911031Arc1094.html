annual ragtime ball set for nov 16 news release 10 31 91 contact stanford university news service 415 723 2558 annual ragtime ball set for nov 16 stanford the third annual ragtime ball sponsored by friends of dance at stanford will be held from 8 pm to midnight saturday nov 16 in roble dance studio 375 santa teresa st the event featuring the paul price society orchestra raises scholarship funds for stanford dance students peninsula dance teacher stan isaacs will act as master of ceremonies and offer instruction in the charleston two step castle walk and tango between 8 and 9 pm at that point the orchestra will take over and play songs from the teens and 20s for three hours stanford's dunai folk ensemble under the direction of jerry duke will perform during intermission tickets can be ordered by calling 415 723 1234 or by writing to friends of dance at stanford 375 santa teresa st stanford ca 94305 tickets also will be sold on campus in white plaza from 11 30 am to 1 30 pm on thursday and friday nov 7 8 and 14 15 ticket cost is $20 in advance $25 at the door and $10 for full time students to prepare for the ball stanford folk dancers will host a ragtime dance lesson by isaacs from 7 30 to 9 30 pm on friday nov 15 in roble gym admission is free with a ticket to the ball or $2 for students and $3 general a mix of rag and folk dancing will follow the lesson for both events participants are asked to wear dance or soft soled shoes kb 911031arc1094 html this is an archived release this release is not available in any other form images mentioned in this release are not available online stanford news service has an extensive library of images some of which may be available to you online direct your request by email to images news service stanford edu stanford university all rights reserved stanford ca 94305 650 723 2300 terms of use copyright complaints

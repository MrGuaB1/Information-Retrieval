alumni elect four new trustees news release 06 16 92 contact stanford university news service 415 723 2558 alumni elect four new trustees stanford four new trustees will join stanford university's governing board this september as a result of alumni balloting stanford alumni association officials announced monday june 15 the winners in the 35 and under category are mariann byerwalter ba 82 32 of san francisco byerwalter is a partner with america first financial corp providing merger and acquisition advice for the financial services industry she is a former chair of the stanford alumni association board michael j perez ba 87 27 of san diego perez is an associate at brobeck phleger and harrison he practices general business litigation and heads the firm's minority recruiting committee the winners in the 36 and over category are ann k bingaman ba 65 llb 68 49 of washington dc bingaman is partner and head of the litigation department at powell goldstein frazer and murphy mernoy e harrison bs 69 mba 74 44 of sacramento harrison is vice president for administration at california state university sacramento runners up in the election were james s backer of valencia calif andrew k brown of bethesda md david o brownwood of greenwich conn and elizabeth chavez of venice calif the next scheduled election is in 1995 nominations may be addressed to the stanford alumni association and a formal call will be issued in the spring of 1994 pr 920616arc2171 html this is an archived release this release is not available in any other form images mentioned in this release are not available online stanford news service has an extensive library of images some of which may be available to you online direct your request by email to images news service stanford edu stanford university all rights reserved stanford ca 94305 650 723 2300 terms of use copyright complaints

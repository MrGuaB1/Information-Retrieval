faculty senate homes in on medical curriculum 1 02 stanford university home stanford news archive stanford report january 16 2002 faculty senate homes in on medical curriculum the school of medicine's faculty senate will meet today at 5 pm to vote on a major change in the clinical curriculum for medical students that has been under discussion for several months the change would replace two electives with required core clerkships in neurology and critical care in addition the new curriculum would combine the current month long clerkships on clinical care basics and ambulatory care into a single two month clerkship that would focus on ambulatory practices senate members will also hear an update on the school's strategic planning initiative from philip pizzo md dean of the school of medicine the meeting will be held in the hospital's bing dining room stanford news stanford homepage stanford university all rights reserved stanford ca 94305 650 723 2300

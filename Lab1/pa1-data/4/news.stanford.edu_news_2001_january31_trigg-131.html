langston trigg named associate vice provost for land and buildings 1 01 stanford report online stanford report january 31 2001 langston trigg named associate vice provost for land and buildings langston trigg has been appointed associate vice provost for land and buildings effective feb 5 he will be responsible for planning programming and construction of capital projects and will report to bob reidy the new vice provost for land and buildings trigg last worked on campus from 1983 to 1991 when he was director of the project to build lucile salter packard children's hospital most recently trigg was associate vice chancellor of capital projects at the university of california berkeley he also has held senior positions in planning and construction at kaiser permanente and the asian art museum trigg is a berkeley graduate reidy named vice provost for land and buildings 1 10 01

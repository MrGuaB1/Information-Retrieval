faculty senate to meet thursday 5 01 stanford report online stanford report may 16 2001 faculty senate to meet thursday the faculty senate will meet thursday at 3 15 pm to take action on a proposal to fulfill the current undergraduate writing and rhetoric requirement through a program in writing and rhetoric that would replace the current program in writing and critical thinking also on the agenda is a proposal to change the undergraduate residency requirement effective this fall the change would require students entering as freshmen to complete a minimum of 135 units at stanford of the 180 units required for a bachelor's degree

helmets required for youth on scooters stanford university home stanford news archive stanford report march 5 2003 helmets required for youth on scooters on the front page of the stanford report feb 26 you ran a picture of an 8 year old boy on campus for parents weekend riding a scooter without a helmet publication of such a picture implies approval of this activity effective jan 1 2003 california law requires all children under the age of 18 years to wear an approved protective helmet when using wheeled transportation this includes bicycles scooters skateboards and in line skates as a major provider of health care in this community and advocates for child safety we must be careful to encourage all people to use proper protective gear and prevent injury david a spain md professor of surgery chief of trauma surgical critical care stanford news stanford homepage stanford university all rights reserved stanford ca 94305 650 723 2300

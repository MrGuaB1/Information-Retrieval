at marcelo stanford edu for an extensive list of resources and services please visit faculty resources frequently asked questions q what's the difference between end of quarter and mid quarter evaluations a the end of quarter evaluations are mandatory and handled by the registrar's office and they do remind students about them those evaluations also have some built in incentives for students since students get to see their grades two weeks earlier if they complete the end of quarter evaluations the midquarter evaluations are optional the center of teaching and learning handles the online system itself to ensure anonymity in the responses and to create the summary reports but the announcement of the evaluation itself is in the hands of the instructor for more details please

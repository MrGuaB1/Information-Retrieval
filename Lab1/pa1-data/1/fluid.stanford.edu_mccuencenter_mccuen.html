peter a mccuen a native of bakersfield mccuen attended stanford university on an eagle scout scholarship graduating at the top of his engineering class he went on to earn a master's degree and a ph d at stanford taking a faculty position in 1960 in 1964 he left to pursue high tech business interests he was most well known for developing verbatim into the world's largest manufacturer of flexible discs mccuen went to sacramento in 1980 where he was involved in local politics philanthropy and business the latter primarily as a developer and builder he died of cancer in november 1999 this high performance computing center was established in his honor by his friends and friends of the environmental fluid mechanics laboratory home mccuen people facilities

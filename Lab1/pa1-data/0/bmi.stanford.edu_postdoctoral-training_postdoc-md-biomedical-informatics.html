choose to combine their clinical fellowship and training in biomedical informatics simultaneously we recommend the clinical training be completed before embarking on your training in biomedical informatics the procedure requires a simultaneous acceptance into a clinical fellowship from the match process and a separate application to the biomedical informatics training program since most clinical fellowships last at least a year or longer you will need to submit your application materials to the academic ms program in biomedical informatics several years in advance of the normal application schedule to ensure a match between their fellowship and matriculation into the bmi program you are encouraged to apply as early as possible since these slots may fill up 2 3 years in

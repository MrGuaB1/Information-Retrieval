pharmacology at stanford from 1967 to 1973 and then served as a consulting professor until his death in 1985 he was president of syntex research in palo alto and was an internationally recognized scientist in the field of the pharmacology and biochemistry of steroids dr dorfman was one of the pioneers in the development of oral contraceptives and the mechanisms of steroid action dr dorfman was a member of the national academy of sciences and the american academy of arts and sciences as well as many other distinguished societies he published more than 650 scientific papers and was the author or editor of 14 books he also founded the journal steroids before coming to syntex and stanford dr dorfman was co director of the worcester foundation for experimental biology during a period of

toward the cost of a continuing stanford undergraduate's summer language study of russian ukrainian albanian czech polish hungarian romanian serbo croatian turkish kazakh kyrgyz or uzbek in an accredited intensive language program no private tutoring will be funded in the us the russian federation ukraine albania the czech republic poland hungary the successor states of the former

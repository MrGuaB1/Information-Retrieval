medicine in vivo microscopy and computer vision for cancer diagnosis and treatment flexible robotics for cardiovascular therapy students and postdocs will work in teams to validate needs prototype devices and test hypotheses via experimental and computational means as appropriate multidisciplinary backgrounds are desirable ranging from bioe me ee cheme cs materials physics or other engineering and biology related disciplines including medical students residents and fellows the key qualification is a passion for innovation and the desire to see through the translation of basic research into practical application ie from invitro to preclinical to human clinic where applicable if you have other ideas and identified needs i am open to entirely new investigations aside from the three main projects outlined above i have dozens of other project ideas from smart helmet design to dental surgery augmentation to brain interface devices for mindfulness none of which have been validated

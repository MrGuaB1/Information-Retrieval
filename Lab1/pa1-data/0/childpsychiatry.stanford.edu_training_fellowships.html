program the brochure will give you a picture of what we do here and also what we hope to accomplish with our residents it will hopefully answer many of your questions as well to be eligible for our program you must have either an md or do degree and have three years of adult residency training in an acgme approved program also you need to have passed the usmle exam steps i ii and iii as well as the ecfmg exam if an international graduate before the interviews an official transcript of your united states medical licensing examination scores is required if you are enrolled in an aamc approved medical school in the us or canada we will accept a copy of your school's report of your scores provided it is verified as a true copy by your registrar and stamped with the school seal there is no minimum score requirement please note that all completed applications are due in our office by october 1 2011 we strongly encourage you to submit your completed applications prior to this date as the interviewing schedule fills quickly we will be interviewing on mondays and wednesdays in september october and november if you have any other questions please contact lawanda mills training programs

project management certificate the stanford advanced project management certificate requires taking three core courses and three elective courses if taking on campus courses exclusively you can complete the certificate by taking 4 courses in march or september and the remaining two at the following on campus session approximately 4 months most participants take a year to complete the certificate online courses are self paced and each course is approximately 20 hours of material if you take the courses full time you can average one course every 2.5 days and thereby complete the certificate in as few as 3 business weeks 15 days most participants take at least 6 months to complete the certificate online the majority of stanford advanced project management participants take a combination of on campus and online courses to earn the certificate do stanford advanced project management courses have exams if taking a course on campus you do not have to take a test however we do require that you are present for the entire course you may only miss 30 minutes for breaks late arrivals and business calls if you do miss more than 30 minutes we require you to do a make up assignment prior to receiving a certificate of completion for the course if taking a course online participants are required to successfully complete an online multiple choice test at the end of each course a score of 85 or higher must be achieved on the test in order to pass the course and be eligible for continuing education units pdus and credit toward the certificate are continuing education units ceus available if you need continuing education units ceus from stanford university in order to maintain your yearly requirements for certain industry certifications or licensures you may

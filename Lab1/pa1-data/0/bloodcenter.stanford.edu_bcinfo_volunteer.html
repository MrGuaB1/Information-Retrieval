volunteer services manager stanford blood center offers a wide variety of volunteer positions and opportunities to participate in a worthwhile program volunteers may choose among positions at our centers in palo alto and mountain view as well as at blood drives located throughout the peninsula and south bay at businesses community organizations schools etc the volunteer program is an integral part of the stanford blood center most of our volunteer positions involve interaction with donors and medical personnel all of our volunteer positions help us achieve our goal of providing blood products to patients in need opportunities include post donation canteen host interact with donors after their donation and monitor their recovery serve donors refreshments label table prepare blood bags for donors label donor medical history sheets donor bags and test tubes and match labeled bags to donors waiting to donate shuttle driver transport blood from blood drives to the lab at the hillview center lab aide help with filing stocking computer entry and other lab related tasks clerical positions assist with mailings filing computer entry and other clerical tasks in various departments please contact tessa moore for more information and to schedule an appointment tessa moore volunteer services manager stanford blood center 3373 hillview avenue palo alto ca 94304 tel 650

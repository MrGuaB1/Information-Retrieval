cannot be overemphasized that it is your responsibility to find a principal advisor the availability of faculty members to serve as principal advisors on dissertations limits the number of students that can be recommended for candidacy even an award of financial support research assistantships traineeships and teaching assistantships for the second or third year of graduate study does not assure that the faculty will recommend approval of your application for candidacy obviously faculty members are more likely to accept the responsibility of supervising the research of a student whose abilities initiative and originality are fairly well known to them you should appreciate that both you and your principal advisor incur some serious commitments in agreeing to work together on a dissertation research project change of advisor or reading committee member if you must change your advisor or a reading committee member inform the department's student services office by filing the

fellow at stanford law school for the stanford center for computers and law codex and a member of codex's stanford intellectual property exchange team sipx the aim of sipx is to ease pressures building from legal and market difficulties surrounding the clearance of copyrights by creating a publicly accessible and dynamic copyright negotiation and contracting platform originally a jazz musician with a bfa from york university franny was drawn to copyright and digital rights communication issues and earned an ll b from queen's university and an ll m from stanford law school's law science and technology program she is an intellectual property litigator with cassels brock & blackwell llp in toronto canada whose practice involves intellectual property digital rights and internet matters in the entertainment media and communications industries in particular she works on collective copyright tariff certifications and royalty licensing applications before the copyright board of canada in ottawa franny was formerly an intern with the copyright board assisting with certification proceedings orphan works applications policy initiatives and administration of copyright collecting

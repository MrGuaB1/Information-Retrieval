huaju macbeth 8 set huaju macbeth 8 set the set is a classic early modernist stage design of the gordon craig or adolph apia type steps are surmounted by towering dark coloured flats and pillars while these dwarf the actors in front they also throw them into strong relief the design encourages strong colour contrasts and lighting effects the director xu xiaozhong has said that the two dominant colours in this production are black and red suggested by the night and blood imagery of the text though this type of set and lighting design were long known in the west by the 1970s it is worth pointing out that they were completely fresh to chinese eyes this set is among the few pioneer abstract stage designs in huaju drama which had mostly made use of pictorial flats

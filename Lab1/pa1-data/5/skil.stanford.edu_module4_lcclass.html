arranges materials by subjects knowing the letter s for your subject area gives you a place to start browsing the shelves check the lc call number page to look up your favorite subject notice that the first letter denotes the larger subject area such as d for history with a second letter designating a subdivision such as dg for rome and italy the table below describes how to read an lc call number 1 books are arranged alphabetically by the initial letters in the call number h451 z682 1998 ha126 i90 1987 hb12 i90 2000 hc13 c55 1952 2 then numerically by the numbers after the letter or letters these numbers are read as whole numbers 1 2 3 101 102 103 1001 1002 1003 etc bf1 j575 bf699 d46 bf1028 b45 1993b bf1125 g38 1992 3 the letters that follow the decimal point are read alphabetically

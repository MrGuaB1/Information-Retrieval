introduction deguchi norio's 3 productions deguchi norio's three productions of a midsummer night's dream in 1994 deguchi norio directed 3 productions of a midsummer night's dream simultaneously for the shakespeare company in the tokyo globe theatre the three productions each with the same cast of actors but in different settings or backdrop contexts were staged consecutively over a single month for the first productions the setting was a school school version and for the second a bar bar version the third production used no setting or background context at all instead the production was staged virtually in the round with the actors wearing half masks mask version

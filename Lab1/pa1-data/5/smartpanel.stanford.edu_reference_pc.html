smartpanel pc projection smart panel tutorial overview how do i quick reference troubleshooting contact us classrooms pc projection to connect a pc to the smartpanel you will need the following items video output port usually found at the back of your pc male to male vga monitor cable sound cable optional required only if you desire audio you must connect your pc to the smartpanel before turning on your computer if you turn on your pc prior to connecting it to the smartpanel your computer will not send a video output signal to the smartpanel and no signal will be projected on the classroom screen some classrooms are equipped with smartboxes which provide a pull up shelf on which to place your laptop pc for easy connection to the smartpanel instructions 1 connect your pc to the smartpanel using a vga to vga monitor cable hd15 male to male 2 if you desire audio eg you are running a cd rom with sound or video connect the audio cables from your pc to the left and right audio jacks labeled l and r 3 turn on the smartpanel system power 4 lower the screen to the proper level 5 start up your computer 6 adjust your screen resolution if necessary if the image appearing on your computer monitor is not projecting onto the screen correctly eg it is cut off unclear or does not project at all you need to adjust your monitor's resolution to match that of the projector in your classroom 7 make sure the room is properly dimmed 8 when you are through disconnect your computer and turn the smartpanel power off home overview how do i quick reference troubleshooting contact us classrooms

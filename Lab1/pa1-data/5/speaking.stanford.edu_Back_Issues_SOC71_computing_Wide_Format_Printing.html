wide format printing resnet survey multimedia course expands bookstore computer store wide format printing in meyer library web view print view by david futey please note this article now contains out of date information since it was written in april 2006 for the latest information about wide format printing in meyer library see http meyer stanford edu posters academic computing offers a wide format poster printing service through meyer library's technology services desk on the second floor patrons can drop off their edited and prepared files at the desk and retrieve their print upon completion it is recommended that patrons make a backup of their file prior to submitting it for print poster advertising meyer's wide format printing service the epson 9600 printer can output posters and fully archival fine art prints up to 44 wide prints are restricted to 55 long to ensure successful printing samples of these prints are on display on the second floor of meyer library patrons can choose to edit their files either on their own computer or on a workstation in the multimedia studio also located on meyer library's second floor these files can then be dropped off at the technology services desk where a trained consultant will do final prep and output to the printer each print is charged to your stanford card plan or copy card account at a flat rate of $50 which includes a 48 mailing tube for transport and storage please note that no refunds will be issued for any non printer errors such as misspelled words or dust on scans prints are ready within 8 hours if dropped off before noon orders placed after noon will be ready by noon the following business day rush processing and other special requests are also available at an additional fee copyright 2004 2006 by the

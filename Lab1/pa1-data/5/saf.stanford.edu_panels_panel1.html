saf 2012 agribusiness agriculture while a relatively risky sector provides a significant number of jobs in african countries at all levels of economic development and presents a number of exciting opportunities this panel will focus on the role the african agriculture sector has played and can play in economic development across the continent it will have representation from agribusinesses financing firms and donors focused on smallholder farmer benefit the discussion will focus on challenges and opportunities in the sector for commercial and economic benefit the environmental and social considerations implications of foreign purchases of agricultural land sustainable sourcing trends value addition processing and global food security dates and times sat feb 25 2012

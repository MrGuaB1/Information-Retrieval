sebastian thrun's homepage homepage research students courses robots papers videos press talks faq cv lab travel contact personal links learning occupancy grids with forward models by sebastian thrun this paper presents a new way to acquire occupancy grid maps with mobile robots virtually all existing occupancy grid mapping algorithms decompose the high dimensional mapping problem into a collection of one dimensional problems where the occupancy of each grid cell is estimated independently of others this induces conflicts that can lead to inconsistent maps this paper shows how to solve the mapping problem in the original high dimensional space thereby maintaining all dependencies between neighboring cells as a result maps generated by our approach are often more accurate than those generated using traditional techniques our approach relies on a rigorous statistical formulation of the mapping problem using forward models it employs the expectation maximization algorithm for estimating maps and a laplacian approximation to determine uncertainty available for download in gzipped postscript and pdf inproceedings thrun01b author thrun s title learning occupancy grids with forward models year 2001 booktitle proceedings of the conference on intelligent robots and systems iros 2001 address hawaii

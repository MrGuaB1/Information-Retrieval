find a person alumni lane library ways to give find a person pgsp stanford psy d consortium the pacific graduate school of psychology stanford university doctor of psychology consortium program offers doctoral level training in clinical psychology leading to the psy d degree the program is run jointly by palo alto university and the stanford university department of psychiatry and behavioral sciences the program's emphases include science competency to train critically reflective informed consumers of psychology and associated clinical sciences clinical competency to train highly skilled clinical psychologists whose clinical practice is grounded on an informed appreciation of the scientific literature cultural competency to train culturally competent clinical psychologists collaborative competency to train psychologists who are proficient in psychological consultation supervision and multidisciplinary collaboration professional competency to train psychologists whose conduct exemplifies the highest levels of professionalism for more information on the program please visit the palo alto university web site you are here stanford medicine school of medicine

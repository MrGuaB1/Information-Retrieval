leave of absence policy and procedures as well as any department requirements fellowship support from the first 4 quarters related to an approved leave of absence are automatically reserved for later use provided the support can be used in the first five years of graduate study any summer quarter without enrollment that precedes or follows an approved leave is counted as one of the four quarters the period considered the first five years of graduate study for the use of the sgf is extended up to 4 quarters related to an approved leave of absence if the fellow does not return after 4 consecutive quarters without enrollment related to a leave the remainder of the fellowship will be cancelled if however the leave of absence is due to serious illness or other medical condition or

the amplitude of a sunspot cycle during the time before sunspot minimum relationships have been found between the size of the next cycle maximum and the length of the previous cycle as well as the level of activity at sunspot minimum and the size of the previous cycle among the most reliable techniques are those that use the measurements of changes in the earth's magnetic field at and before

events bizpartners supports partners and families of mba students bizpartners student clubs tips from sos about life at stanford i found relocating to stanford to be my easiest transition yet in the friends department my husband and i have relocated before and i remember feeling lonely and lost at the gsb it was the exact opposite we almost had too many invitations for social gatherings it was really reassuring to me that many of the sos were in the same boat moving there and not having a pre established community generally speaking everyone wants to get to know each other and establish a community so events are not exclusive and the motto is always the more the merrier chandler murphy with husband tim murphy mba 2008 more profiles families & partners spouses partners and families are an integral part of the stanford gsb community and have access to many of the gsb's organizations and facilities and are encouraged to participate in school and student sponsored activities once admitted to stanford you will receive a full packet of information that will help you and your family or partner transition to stanford including childcare resources educational resources schools colleges universities employment opportunities volunteer opportunities biz partners biz partners formally known as the stanford business partners association is a group formed specifically for partners and families biz partners offers opportunities to make friends socialize gather resources for finding jobs and pursuing personal interests and enhances the overall gsb experience for families and partners the group coordinates small group dinners tennis tournaments cooking classes book and movie clubs tailgates and plenty of other fun opportunities to connect with other families they also offer a working partners network a support group for partners who are working or job hunting the group welcomes men and women of all ethnic and cultural backgrounds regardless of sexual orientation with children or without employed inside or outside the home biz kids biz kids is a subset of biz partners just for partners and students with children biz kids organizes outings for children and their parents such as trips to local parks museums zoos and holiday parties they can also help coordinate age appropriate playgroups find babysitters and childcare resources and locate parent support

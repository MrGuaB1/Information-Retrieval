program access to critical dialogues the arthur and toni rembe rock center for corporate governance at stanford university was created with the vision to advance the understanding and practice of corporate governance this is most likely to occur in a cross disciplinary environment where financial experts lawyers regulators investors practitioners and academics can meet and exchange ideas the corporate governance roundtable a joint initiative of the stanford graduate school of business and stanford law school seeks to collaborate with organizations that will join as members and actively participate in the practice and study of corporate governance as a member of the corporate governance roundtable your organization will benefit from access to center conferences panels forums and other events members will not only have the opportunity to make meaningful connections with faculty and executives from other firms but will also engage with top business and law students for more

course reserves the branner library provides reserve services for the school of earth sciences checking out reserve materials consult the online reserves catalog or the reserves loose leaf binder at the branner circulation desk includes processing status information to identify items on reserve present current id to use materials most reserve materials circulate for two hours note carefully the expected return time when checking out reserves reserve materials may be renewed by phone if no one is waiting for them they may be checked out overnight one hour before the library closes and returned within the first hour after opening on the following day please return reserve materials directly to branner's circulation desk because ready and equal access to reserve materials is critical to the academic enterprise stiff fines are assessed for tardy responses to items recalled for reserves and for reserve items kept beyond their loan period we require your prompt action in helping make

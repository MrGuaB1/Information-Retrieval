dining pavillion at the gsb's knight management center it's difficult to save enough money to completely fund the substantial investment it takes to go to business school the gsb financial aid office works with all students to make sure they receive the appropriate type and level of financial aid so that everyone can afford not only go to business school but also to participate in the myriad of extracurricular opportunities brian bilnoski mba 2006 texas usa associate crossbow ventures filing for aid after you are admitted to the mba program and have completed the financial aid application process the financial aid office reviews your income and assets to determine your financial need and provides a package of fellowships and or loans there are a variety of fellowships and loans available including some non need based loans financial need is evaluated annually to ensure a fair and equitable distribution of aid we strongly suggest that you do your best to repay consumer debt before you apply for financial aid we will not be able to decrease the value of your assets by your amount of consumer debt after you have applied for aid we will send you detailed information for

linear collider r&d opportunities workshop at slac linear collider r&d opportunities workshop at slac last update wed may 8 10 39 49 pdt 2002 a workshop on linear collider r&d opportunities will be held at slac on may 31 2002 its goal is to facilitate university community involvement in lc detector and accelerator r&d this workshop is aimed at those who missed the recent meetings at fnal and cornell but want to learn how to get involved it will build on the results from those meetings talks will cover outstanding r&d issues status of ongoing r&d efforts and r&d funding possibilities time and discussion will be allotted to generating seeds for proposals and coordinating with other efforts all are welcome details about the workshop including a tentative agenda and registration information are on the web details on workshop looking forward to seeing you at slac john jaros on behalf of the slac lc working group

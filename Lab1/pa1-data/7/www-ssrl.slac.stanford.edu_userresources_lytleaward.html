executive committee to promote important technical or scientific accomplishments in synchrotron radiation based science and to foster collaboration and efficient use of beam time among users and staff at ssrl the lytle award consists of a certificate and $1000 awardee names are added to a plaque displayed in the ssrl user research administration office the following people have received the farrel

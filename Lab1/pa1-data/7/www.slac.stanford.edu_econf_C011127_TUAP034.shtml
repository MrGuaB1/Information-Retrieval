icalepcs paper id 1015 icalepcs 2001 abstracts tuap034 poster presenter frank locci cern email frank locci cern ch review status proceedings ready 01 15 02 fulltext pdf eprint physics 0111190 rejuvenation of the controls of the cern isolde facility using industrial components j allard o jonsson f locci g mornacchi cern the ps controls group launched in april 2000 a project to provide a new front end infrastructure for the isolde radioactive beam facility at cern industrial components were introduced with the intention of deploying them later on as standard components of the control system for the whole of the ps complex this paper describes the new isolde controls infrastructure with emphasis on the use of the profibus fieldbus distributed i o modules and programmable logic controllers plc the use of ethernet as communication medium to connect the vme lynxos based ps front ends of the present control system to the plcs is also described a proposal for applying the isolde approach to the ps controls is presented in particular in view of future cern projects such as ctf3 and leir to conclude we make suggestions on how to extend the proposed solution to applications where real time response is an issue econf c011127 eproceedings committees participants program summary icalepcs 2001 econf last updated september 13 2002 website problem contact technical publications slac

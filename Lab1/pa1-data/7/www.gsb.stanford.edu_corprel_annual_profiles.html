strong partnership with the david and lucile packard foundation has made a significant impact on the creation and growth of a number of programs at the gsb the foundation was instrumental in the establishment of the school's center for social innovation with a founding gift a decade ago and continues to further the center's mission to build and strengthen the capacity of individuals and organizations to develop innovative solutions to social problems through management prowess the packard foundation also has supported the stanford management internship fund smif a program that inspires our mba students to pursue their passion for nonprofit work through summer internships while easing the financial implications of that choice with its current fellowship support for nonprofit industry leaders in the school's sloan master's program the foundation aids organizations around the world by furthering vital management and leadership skills the sloan experience was truly transformational for me as a leader in the nonprofit sector and would not have been possible without the support of the packard foundation the combination of new skills relationships and confidence along with the intangible benefits that are part of the magic of being at stanford have had a profound impact on my leadership and ability to impact social change becky crowe hill sloan fellow 10

scientific advisory committee sac reports to and advises the ssrl director on issues related to operation of ssrl as a scientific user facility planning construction and operation of new ssrl members are appointed by the ssrl director for 3 year terms with one third of the members rotating off and being replaced every year membership may be extended eg to stagger rotation periods of members the sac chair is appointed by the ssrl director meetings the sac meets periodically as needed at minimum once a year the meeting agenda is set by the ssrl director in close coordination with the sac chair before each meeting the ssrl director will outline a specific charge to the sac and the advice sought from the sac reporting the advice of the sac is conveyed to ssrl management at the

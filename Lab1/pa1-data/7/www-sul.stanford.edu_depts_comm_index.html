communications sulair communications our mission the mission of the sulair communications group is to ensure that members of the sulair organization the stanford community and the broader public are well informed about sulair resources services and ongoing initiatives in accomplishing this mission the sulair communications group is responsible for providing managerial oversight of the sulair web site and maintaining many top level pages of the site including the home page publishing the brief guide to sulair speaking of computers sulair news and other publications as needed maintaining a news & events web site for sulair designing and managing publicity to create awareness of ongoing sulair initiatives with a special focus on digital initiatives and technology providing publishing and editorial support to other sulair publications and web sites ensuring that sulair is appropriately represented in stanford wide publications and web sites including the stanford web site the computing and communications web site the stanford bulletin and approaching stanford developing and coordinating a patron assessment program to include administering analyzing and publishing regular patron surveys working with appropriate organizations across campus eg itss university communications to facilitate the dissemination of information and publicity about sulair facilities and services contact

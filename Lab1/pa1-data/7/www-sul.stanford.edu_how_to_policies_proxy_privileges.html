users circulation policies interlibrary lending proxy privileges reference policies access to electronic resources printer friendly printer friendly how to policies policies proxy privileges the purpose of the proxy privilege the stanford university libraries permits the faculty of the university to have proxies who may represent them in the libraries this privilege is meant to assist faculty in doing their library research by allowing their designees to use the library in their name the library proxy card gives the bearer proxy status ie the privilege of charging out library materials in the name of a specified faculty member who is eligible for the proxy privilege the proxy privilege may be extended to regular stanford university faculty to emeritus faculty to stanford appointed visiting professors and to academic staff with a provostial teaching appointment a faculty member may sponsor two proxies the need for an exception to this should be brought to the attention of the head of the privileges office if needed one proxy may serve more than one faculty member who may serve as a proxy regular stanford university employees registered stanford students and others hired as research assistants are eligible to serve as proxies how to apply for the proxy privilege applications for the proxy card may be obtained at the privileges office in green library proxy cards may be issued for periods from one month to one year at the sponsoring faculty member's discretion the proxy card application form is available in two formats a downloadable pdf file a print version available at the privileges desk in green library adobe acrobat is required for downloading this form you can download the adobe acrobat reader here library use by the proxy the proxy card may only be used in support of the faculty member served by the proxy sponsoring faculty members are responsible for all materials borrowed in their name all circulation notices will be sent to the faculty member library materials borrowed on the proxy card will fall due no later than the expiry date of the proxy privilege if abuse of the proxy privilege occurs the privilege will be suspended or revoked as appropriate until the matter is resolved approved by the academic council committee on libraries may 20 1976 contact information privileges office green library stanford california 94305 6063 hours

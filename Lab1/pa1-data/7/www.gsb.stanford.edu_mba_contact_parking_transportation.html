permits you can buy in advance if you are planning to attend a special event on campus there are daily permits designed just for you you can purchase permits at the parking and transportation services offices by mail or online daily visitor permits are also available for purchase at several other locations on campus public transportation by train caltrain is a commuter rail service that runs between gilroy south and san francisco north there are two stops close to stanford one on california avenue and another closer station at the end of palm drive in downtown palo alto the free stanford shuttle called marguerite meets most trains at both the palo alto and california ave stations from 6 am to 7 45 pm monday friday from points south of stanford the santa clara valley transportation agency vta runs buses to palo alto and most points south of stanford it also operates the light rail line in santa clara county from points north of stanford samtrans runs buses to most points north of stanford five of samtrans lines serve stanford from the east bay the dumbarton express used by many east bay commuters runs weekdays from the union city bay area rapid transit bart station across

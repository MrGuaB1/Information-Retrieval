spear3 has been operating at 350 ma since june 2011 the current will likely increase during the run to a maximum of 500 ma users will be notified prior to an increase in the current ssrl employs a frequent fill schedule to maintain the spear3 current approximately constant automatic injections will be conducted every 5 minutes automatic injections will only occur at the designated 5 minute intervals ie on the hour and every 5 minutes thereafter if the injector is not functional at the designated fill time then the fill will be skipped the current will be replenished at the next scheduled fill time after the injector is repaired and normal injection intervals will resume the operator will give an audible announcement prior to first injection if the current has decreased more

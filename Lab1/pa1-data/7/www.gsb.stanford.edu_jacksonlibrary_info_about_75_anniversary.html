experience jackson library looks ahead from stanford gsb news april 2008 jackson library was formally inaugurated on april 3 1933 some seven years after the founding of the business school the new facility was located in a room in the quad that measured 30 feet by 40 and held about 1000 volumes and tables for sixty students in addition to the core collection the library contained corporate reports volumes of leading business journals and various other research materials many supposedly transferred directly from dean jackson's own collection in 1966 the library moved to its present central location thanks in part to the energy and dedication of longtime director marion smith the collections continued to grow focusing on the teaching needs of the school as it evolved into the premier institution of general management that it is today as in the past jackson library will continue to grow and change to support the students and scholars of the 21st century on april 23 2008 jackson library commemorated its past and toasted the future with a gala celebration a message from dean robert l joss i am very sorry to be in india talking about the gsb of course while the 75th

users circulation policies interlibrary lending policies policies printer friendly printer friendly how to borrow get access visiting scholars definition stanford university defines visiting scholars as academic and non student researchers who are invited by a school department or other stanford entity to visit the university without being paid as faculty members or employees hold a doctoral degree or are recognized experts in their fields must be resident at stanford for a minimum of 1 quarter contribute to stanford's academic and research mission by lecturing or actively collaborating with stanford faculty or students on current stanford research scholars who do not meet all these criteria may still be eligible for some level of privileges refer to the other scholars section below library privileges for visiting scholars obtaining an id card researchers who meet the university visiting scholar requirements are eligible for stanford university id cards id cards are issued at the stanford card office located in forsythe hall library privileges stanford id cards give you access to the sulair libraries they do not automatically grant borrowing privileges to apply for borrowing privileges you will need to visit the privileges desk in green library state your status as a visiting scholar and present the university issued id card provide permanent and local address information sign a form agreeing to follow sulair policies and return books note visiting scholars must present a stanford id card at any sulair library to borrow materials sulair will no longer issue internal id cards for these patrons you must obtain your card from the stanford card office spouses domestic partners the spouse or same sex domestic partner of a visiting scholar may obtain access privileges with the same expiration date by applying at the privileges desk library privileges for other scholars visiting scholars who are not eligible for a university id have the option to use the libraries as a visitor or apply for a library card visiting the library you are eligible for 7 days of free access visits to the libraries during a 12 month period starting the day you register as a visitor visits need not be taken concurrently further visits may be purchased see our visitor access policy for details applying for a library card if you feel there is a demonstrated scholarly need for you to obtain access or borrowing privileges you may apply by filling out the visiting scholar application form available at the privileges desk during library hours please fill out the form completely you must fill in your information including permanent and local address you must sign the form indicating that you will follow sulair policies and return books the head of the department sponsoring your visit must fill in their section and sign the form to sponsor your privileges bring the completed form in person to the privileges desk during loan desk hours note we no longer accept the paper visiting scholar cards from departments for this purpose you must submit a signed letter to be considered for privileges contact information privileges office green library stanford california 94305

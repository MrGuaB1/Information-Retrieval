chang kao has accepted slac's offer to become the next director for the stanford synchrotron radiation lightsource chi chang has been the nsls chairperson since 2006 and the director for the bnl joint photon sciences institute since 2008 he brings extensive scientific and management background to ssrl and slac his own research is focused in the area of condensed matter physics and materials science

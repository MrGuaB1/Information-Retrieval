healthy eating goals goals in providing this site to the stanford student community we hope to increase health and nutrition awareness provide students with access to resources increase our knowledge of the health and well being of stanford students we hope you will use this site as a starting point for seeking out health and nutrition information the resources compiled here can lead you to some of the best nutrition information available both through the web and on campus our ultimate goal is to provide stanford students with well designed meaningful health and wellness programming and resources students who choose to complete the self analysis survey will be agreeing to submit their anonymous scores to our database the database will be used to provide a snapshot of student health allowing us to better serve the needs of stanford students

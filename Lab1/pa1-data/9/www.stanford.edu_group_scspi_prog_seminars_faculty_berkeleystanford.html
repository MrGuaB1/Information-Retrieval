future of inequality trend data open beta available berkeley stanford inequality seminars the berkeley stanford inequality seminar established in 2006 07 by professor sam lucas dept of sociology uc berkeley and professor paula england dept of sociology stanford university provides an opportunity for faculty in the two universities to come together to discuss issues of poverty and inequality the format is one of high participation much give and take and a shared commitment to making sense of the processes and dynamics underlying poverty and inequality the typical presentation in this seminar is not a lecture not even a talk but a spirited dialogue between the speaker and the other participants 2010 11 schedule november 10 2010 martin sanchez jankowski december 1 2010 daniel mcfarland

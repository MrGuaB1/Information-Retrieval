lil cryopump chamber facility sppl home tour sppl lil cryopump chamber facility this test facility consists of a non magnetic stainless steel chamber approximately 0.6 m in diameter and 1.2 m in length the chamber is pumped by a single 50 cm diameter cryopump cvi tm500 backed by a 60 l s mechanical pump the base pressure of the facility is approximately 10 4 pa as measured by an ionization gauge uncorrected for xenon thruster testing at xenon flow rates of 0.12 0.20 mg s results in chamber background pressure of approximately 10 3 pa

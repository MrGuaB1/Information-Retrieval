arbor press arbor press the arbor press is one of the first things you see when you walk into the machine shop its handle resembles the steering wheel of a ship but it shouldn t be used as such trying to spin the wheel quickly in an attempt to force a press fit can damage the press as evidenced by the several missing nubs on the handle the press is designed to apply force not impulse to achieve press fits if you have questions about how to best achieve a press fit using the arbor press please see a ta the arbor press is a mechanical advantage press and as such the danger lies in crushing parts of your body during operation particularly your fingers other less frequent problems stem from buckling and breaking of parts and their subsequent shooting out of the press at high velocity

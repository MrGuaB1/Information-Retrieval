cecile andrews on campus author cecile andrews cecile andrews author of a circle of simplicity return to the good life recently spent two years on the stanford campus as an affiliated scholar with the institute for research on women and gender twenty some years ago she received her doctorate in education from stanford after that she worked as an administrator for a community college until she focused on simplicity study circles she wrote the book the circle of simplicity currently she continues to head the simplicity circle project for the non profit organization seeds of simplicity a program of the center for religion ethics social policy at cornell university a comprehensive summary of the her work can be found on her national website that site allows you to search their database and find a study group or circle near you and you can learn about or join the on line study group home click news for possible upcoming events author cecile andrews's views author bill jensen's views simplicity circles stories contact person on campus last updated march 10 2003

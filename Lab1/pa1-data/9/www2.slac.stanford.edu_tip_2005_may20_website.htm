launched a new web site that highlights our varied scientific initiatives as well as important information for staff users and the public the new slac home page a key goal of the new web site is to communicate the excitement and energy surrounding the science we do at the lab this has meant creating new content and imagery as well as organizing existing pages around the various audiences that

human viruses at stanford university welcome to the stanford human viruses website founded in the winter of 1998 this site offers an unparalleled resource for basic information regarding human viruses constructed by a group of 40 undergraduates at stanford university it is our aim to provide a general overview of each human viral family with links to more detailed pages regarding specifics of molecular biology pathology epidemiology etc we apologize for the absense of information for poxviridae and retroviridae unfortunately we have not acquired appropriate detail to post these pages this page will continue to be supported by stanford undergraduates studying virology and we will do our best to keep abreast of current issues for your reference we have provided the most recent date of revision on the main page of every viral family we appreciate any comments or recommendations you may have regarding our site samuel williams and robert siegel

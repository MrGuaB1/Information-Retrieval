stanford surf club home join surf forecast surf etiquette events contact us joining the surf club there are a few things you ll need to do to join the surf club have at least 1 year of surfing experience email michael taylor at taylormd stanford edu about your interest to join take a swim test 100 yds tread water for 2 min take a short written test to show you know how to surf receive a short instructional on how to secure surfboards to roof racks sign a liability waiver most important is to email us about your interest then we will help organize a time when you can take care of the rest of your requirements once you have completed the above items you will be put on the surf club email list receive email updates on upcoming surf sessions and be able to go out surfing with the club

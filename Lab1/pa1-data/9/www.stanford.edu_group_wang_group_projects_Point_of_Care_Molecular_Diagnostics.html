point of care molecular diagnostics stanford center for magnetic nanotechnology the wang group point of care molecular diagnostics and radiation exposure traige home projects publications people news group photos principal investigator shan x wang sponsors gates foundation barda our goal is to develop an easy to use no skilled technicians required and ultraportable device no laboratory equipment necessary that is capable of immediate sensitive specific and multiplex protein assays for point of care poc screening of infectious disease at an affordable cost the project includes developing biochemistry portable electronics and microfluidics for medical diagnostics in addition we are developing a fast cheap and accurate technology for determining the level of radiation exposure victims might suffer in a radiological incident the technology would be able to sort through blood samples of victims on the scene swiftly as well as in the lab looking for concentrations of particular proteins or biomarkers that reliably reflect the dose of radiation a person has received

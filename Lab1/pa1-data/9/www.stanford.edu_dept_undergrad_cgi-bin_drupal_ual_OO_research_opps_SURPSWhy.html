feedback why participate in the symposia of undergraduate research and public service surps and admit weekend surps asurps presenting your academic creative and public service work in a formal setting is a mark of true accomplishment in your undergraduate studies it is also outstanding preparation for graduate school applications and other future presentations all presenters may include their presentations on resumes graduate school and fellowship applications you will also gain valuable experience articulating ideas about your projects through posters or other visual display presentations involving verbal and graphic forms of explanation this experience is greatly enhanced if you participate in the pre symposium workshop on creating combined oral and visual representations of your work the symposia will be held on thursday of reunion homecoming weekend and saturday of admit weekend we anticipate that the surps audience will include stanford faculty students and staff and alumni the asurps audience will include prospective stanford students and their families rather than alumni this diverse audience should provide an excellent forum for networking and conversations about career

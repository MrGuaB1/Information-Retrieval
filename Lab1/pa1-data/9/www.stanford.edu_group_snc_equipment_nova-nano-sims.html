seven masses in parallel from the same small volume in energy research nanosims can characterize the nanostructured materials with complex composition that are increasingly important candidates for energy generation and storage stanford's nanosims is one of only 22 nanosims in the world and one of only a very few of the most current versions of this instrument only a handful of nanosimss worldwide can be accessed through collaboration with the owners as stanford's can more detail secondary ion mass spectrometry sims is a technique used to analyze the composition of solid surfaces and thin films by sputtering the surface of the specimen with a focused primary ion beam and collecting and analyzing ejected secondary ions these secondary ions are measured with a mass spectrometer to determine the elemental isotopic or molecular composition of the surface sims is a very sensitive surface analysis technique being able to detect elements present in the parts per billion range the cameca nanosims has a uniquely designed column a lens for ions that can focus the primary ion beam down to a 50nm spot to provide high lateral resolution analysis the nanosims also employs a multi

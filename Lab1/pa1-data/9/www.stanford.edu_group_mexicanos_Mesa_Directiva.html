mesa directiva the mexican student association unites a large and diverse group of mexican students at stanford university and acts as a voice for issues which concern them mexican students at our university have a wide variety of interests but also share a common bond in their heritage and culture our organization uses these common factors to bring the mexican academic community closer together through a variety of academic social and cultural events our goal is to provide a network for students of all backgrounds who share an interest in issues related to mexico our academic social and cultural events will play an important role in increasing the presence of mexico in the stanford community mesa directiva 2008 2009 presidente president daniel lopez daniello stanford edu vice presidente vice president pablo garc a del real gdelreal stanford edu tesorero treasurer calendar officer noriega stanford edu webmaster andy ehrensberger aehrens stanford edu

veterans selecting the appropriate application plan selecting the appropriate application plan freshman or transfer status an important aspect to consider when applying for undergraduate admission to any selective university is whether to apply as an incoming freshman or a transfer student to learn more about transfer eligibility requirements go to the eligibility and credit transfer webpage to apply to stanford as a freshman applicant you must not have enrolled in a degree seeking program at another institution of higher learning it is our policy to review all coursework that you have taken since the beginning of high school which may include military sponsored language or technical skill coursework there are also limits on the number of transferable units you may accrue prior to applying application suggestions our application requirements section of the website contains a complete set of guidelines meant to help you become familiar with the application process be sure to note our application deadlines as well as the specific requirements about standardized testing recommendation letters and transcripts however included below are some points that may help guide you as you prepare to apply testing requirement regardless of whether you apply as a freshman or a transfer standardized testing is an important requirement for all applicants please note that while we do still accept scores on the 1600 sat scale many applicants feel their current level of academic preparedness is better demonstrated by a more recent sat or act plus writing teacher evaluations in addition to the two evaluations we require from college instructors we welcome and invite an additional recommendation from someone who might know you from a context that is not strictly academic because we are interested in how you will contribute to the university as a whole a former supervisor commanding officer or fellow community service volunteer can often strengthen and highlight aspects that are not sufficiently captured by the general application transcripts although our review of your academic record will include your high school career it is important to understand that your more recent performance will carry the most weight in our evaluation process what you accomplished in high school may no longer be indicative of the intellectual maturity or dedication to study that you now possess instead your experience and the opportunities you have pursued may demonstrate to a greater extent what we gain in your candidacy we are very interested in understanding the full spectrum of your academic journey and hope that each applicant works to ensure our office is seeing the whole and complete picture of their academic background pdf documents require the free

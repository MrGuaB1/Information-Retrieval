urban styles about in the year 2000 undergraduates abe estess and rhiannon meier founded urban styles to provide an opportunity for stanford students to learn and perform jazz routines urban styles dance company is the only group on campus dedicated to la style jazz dancing in the group's routines urban styles strives to combine personal style and flair with technically advanced jazz skills urban styles offers its members the opportunity to choreograph pieces for the group music selection ranges from pop artists to broadway musicals style ranges from contemporary to thrash jazz urban styles performs at a wide variety of venues throughout the academic year including stanford's orientation o show an art affair cardinal ballet's urban nights jam pac d's hip hop extravaganza dv8's encounterculture admit weekend dance expo as well as our own spring show recently titled elements held each may since 2004

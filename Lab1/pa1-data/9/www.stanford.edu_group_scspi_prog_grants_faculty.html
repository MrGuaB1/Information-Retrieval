inequality trend data open beta available faculty grants the center awards one or more research grants each year to support stanford university faculty carrying out research on poverty and inequality the grants are small up to $5000 and are intended as start up funds to assist in the development of grant proposals and new and innovative research programs eligibility awards are made to stanford university faculty who are affiliates of the center for the study of poverty and inequality and hold academic council appointments award amount awards of up to $5000 will be made expenses the grant may be used to cover any research relevant expenses applications applicants should submit a cv a short proposal no more than a page and a budget these materials should be sent to the administrative

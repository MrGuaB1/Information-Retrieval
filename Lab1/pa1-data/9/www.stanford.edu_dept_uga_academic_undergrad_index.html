education general education requirements the general education requirements are an integral part of undergraduate education at stanford all programs of study achieve balance between depth of knowledge acquired through specialization and breadth of knowledge acquired through exploration purpose introduce students to a broad range of fields and areas of study within the humanities social sciences natural sciences applied sciences and technology help students prepare to become responsible members of society introduce students to the major social historical cultural and intellectual forces that mathematics natural sciences and social sciences the education for citizenship requirement provides students with some of the skills and knowledge necessary for citizenship in contemporary national cultures and participation in the global cultures of the 21st century students take one course from two of the following four areas ethical reasoning the global community american cultures and gender

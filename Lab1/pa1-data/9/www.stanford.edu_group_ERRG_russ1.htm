errg russ garber russ garber research statement my research concerns complex engineering systems that fall behind schedule when behind schedule there is a general tendency for contractors agents to cut corners in an attempt to regain the original timetable when an agent takes a shortcut he may be imposing a risk upon the system that upper management is unwilling to accept in a complex system like a space satellite such shortcuts can have a very detrimental affect on system performance i model this problem using influence diagrams and the principal agent format to produce a decision support framework to help upper management set the appropriate constraints for the operators publications garber r and me pate cornell geographic distribution of design teams a probabilistic analysis of reliability effects and a satellite payload illustration submitted to the journal of spacecraft and rockets in april 2002 garber r and b carlson the uncertain supply problem an analysis including disposal cost and a decision analytic perspective submitted to ieee transactions e mail rgarber stanford edu

stanford university papua new guinea medical project the land papua new guinea became an independent nation and a member of the commonwealth of nations in 1975 the total population of papua new guinea in 1990 was estimated to be 3689000 yielding a density of about 8 persons per sq km papua new guinea has an ordered infrastructure divided into a national capital district and 19 provinces port moresby is the capital of the country and its principal city lying just south of the equator papua new guinea has a tropical humid climate the specific locale for stanford's involvement in papua new guinea is the sepik river basin region in the east sepik province ambunti is the administrative and trade center of the region and the location of our educational course component of the project the small village of oum is our base camp site in the bush from which the clinics are organized the island of papua new guinea is a place of unsurpassed natural beauty
